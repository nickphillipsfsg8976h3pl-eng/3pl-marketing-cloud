Realms in Writing Legends

 

Alongside our inspiring writing prompts, we're thrilled to introduce a fantastic world-building feature: Realms!

 

Realms is where students can use their hard-earned coins to craft their very own extraordinary worlds and to get started, we're offering the first Realm 'Farm,' FREE!

 

Whether students are looking to create a cuddly animal farm, build a futuristic city, or embark on a swashbuckling pirate adventure, they'll have a blast building these incredible worlds.
