Our new Placement Test is the gateway to effective, targeted learning, as it automatically adapts to each student's current writing skills.

 

You can find the Placement Test in two areas of the program:
  • When a new student starts with Writing Legends, the Placement Test will automatically be available to them. Students can either take the test or start from Map One.
  • Teachers can assign or re-assign the Placement Test at any point to ensure students are learning at the correct level for their current knowledge.
