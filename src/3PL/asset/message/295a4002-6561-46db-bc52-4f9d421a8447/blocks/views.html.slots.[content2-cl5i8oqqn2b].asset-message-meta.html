You may have heard the term ‘soft skills’ bandied around the place recently. Few criticisms sting as much as being accused that we’re not equipping our students with the skills necessary for the ‘real world’, i.e. the job market. Especially in the area of maths, where it’s not uncommon to hear that all we’re doing is churning out people who are good a memorizing a formula.

Ouch. Truth is that sometimes soft skills do get lost in the mix when we're tied up teaching the hard skills of the mathematics curriculum - but that doesn't mean we've forgotten about them. Have a read of our blog "Finding Soft Skills in Mathematics Isn't as Hard as you Might Think" to find out the small tweaks to bring soft skills back into focus in your classroom.
