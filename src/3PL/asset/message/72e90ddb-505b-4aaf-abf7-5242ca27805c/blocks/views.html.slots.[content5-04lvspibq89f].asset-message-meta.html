• New Courses has been designed and developed using research-based pedagogy: High Impact Teaching and Explicit teaching.
  • Learning is structured, providing students with explanation, demonstration and practice. All with a focus on producing specific learning outcomes.
  • New Courses covers more than fluency. It takes students further into reasoning and problem-solving through carefully crafted and sequenced sets of questions.
  • The question sets are scaffolded with 5 Fluency, 3 Reasoning, and 2 Problem-Solving questions, accompanied by concept refresh videos, hints, worked solutions and worked example lesson videos.
  • New Courses works in a motivating and highly interactive way for students. It does what Mathletics does best, making learning fun and rewarding throughout their learning journey.
