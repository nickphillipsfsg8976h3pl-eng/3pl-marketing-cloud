1. Log in to your Reading Eggs Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on 'Assignments'
  3. Select 'Create Assignments'
  4. Choose your class or individual students
  5. Set start and finish dates
  6. Pick the year/grade level and corresponding lesson
  7. Click 'Create this assignment'
