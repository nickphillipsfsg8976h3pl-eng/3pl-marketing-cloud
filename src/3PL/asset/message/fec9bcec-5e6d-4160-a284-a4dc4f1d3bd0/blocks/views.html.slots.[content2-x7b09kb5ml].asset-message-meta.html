We’re thrilled to announce a major Brightpath Writing update - Assisted Marking is now available for handwritten work, eliminating the need for manual transcription.

Using advanced Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology, handwritten scripts are automatically digitised with remarkable accuracy, even for early writers or challenging handwriting styles.

Simply scan and upload your handwritten work as you normally would. Our OCR technology will convert the text, enabling efficient processing just like typed documents.

Log in now to try the new feature and start saving time on your assessments!

Coming soon: Enhanced Assisted Marking
We're also upgrading our natural language processing to version 2.0, which will:

  • Provide more accurate suggested scores

  • Better align with expert human judgment

  • Significantly reduce scoring outliers through advanced technology

  • Give you even more confidence in the assisted marking process.

Your expertise remains central

Assisted marking supports but never replaces your professional judgment. It analyses writing conventions to give you a helpful starting point, while you maintain final decision-making.

Need help?
We’ll share a quick training video soon. In the meantime, please reach out to us with any questions.

Warm regards,
The Brightpath Team

P.S. We'd love to hear your feedback on the new handwriting recognition feature!
