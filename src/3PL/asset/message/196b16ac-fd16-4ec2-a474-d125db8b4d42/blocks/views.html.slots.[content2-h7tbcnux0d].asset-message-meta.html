Detailed lesson plans
Each Mathseeds lesson is complemented by a full lesson plan to help support you. It contains a preview of the lesson, how the material correlates to curriculum standards, ideas on additional classroom activities to help support learning, as well as all the Mathseeds resources that tie into the key concept.

These include downloadable worksheets, problem-solving and reasoning activities, classroom posters and more. Go to ‘Lessons’ to see all the materials available to you.
