✍️When a new student starts with Writing Legends, the Placement Test will automatically be available to them. Students can either take the test or start from Map One.

✍️ Alternatively, you can assign (or re-assign) the Placement Test at any time to ensure students are learning at the right level. We’ve even created an interactive walkthrough to guide you through it.

Just click Support in the top right corner of your dashboard, select ‘Assign students a placement test’, then follow the on-screen instructions. Easy!
