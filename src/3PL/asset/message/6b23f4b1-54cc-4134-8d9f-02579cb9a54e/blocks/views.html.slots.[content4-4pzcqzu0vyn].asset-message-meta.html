How to access reports:

  1. Log into your Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Click on 'Reports' in the left menu
  3. Select ‘Student Reports’
  4. Select the class, group, individual student, or Select All
  5. Choose which report you wish to view


Whether it's tracking participation, skill mastery, or Live Mathletics challenges, Mathletics reports are your guiding light and can be exported and downloaded for easy record keeping.
