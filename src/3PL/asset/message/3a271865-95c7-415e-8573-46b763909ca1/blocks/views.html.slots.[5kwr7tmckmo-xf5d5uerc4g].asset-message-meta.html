Does Reading Eggspress have a Placement Test?


No matter which program(s) you choose to use with your students, they should first complete a Placement Test to ensure they’re starting their journey at the appropriate level.

If a student chooses to start their Reading Eggs journey, they will be asked to complete a Placement Test or begin their journey on Lesson 1 (no reading skills).

In Reading Eggspress, students are required to take a Placement Test before they can begin. In both programs, the student will be placed on the map best suited to their level after answering three questions wrong.
