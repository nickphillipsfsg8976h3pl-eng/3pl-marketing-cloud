What awaits your students in #Books:

  1. Select '#Books' from the Student Home screen.
  2. Explore 'Book Blurbs' and choose a captivating book cover.
  3. Identify 5 impactful Wow Words.
  4. Compare their Wow Words with the trending top 10 words.
  5. Rate their interest based on the cover and summary.
  6. Select 'My Preferences' to view their preferred books, neatly organised.
  7. Discover 'Wow Words' to see their selected words and chosen books!
