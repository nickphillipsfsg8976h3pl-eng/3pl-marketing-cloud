Fast Phonics is now the complete synthetic Phonics program to complement your instruction.

  • Students learn letters and sounds in structured and systematic way, progressing through 20 different levels.
  • They love the colourful animations, working at their own pace and the instant feedback with end of level quizzes
  • Teachers get heaps of resources including a teacher guide, worksheets, weekly planners for each letter set, decodable phonics readers and more!
