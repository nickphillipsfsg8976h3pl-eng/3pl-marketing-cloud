1. Log in to your ABC Reading Eggs account.
  2. Go to the Teacher Library.
  3. Filter as needed by Lexile, Fiction, or Nonfiction or,
  4. Search for a set of books that fits your class. Consider a theme based on your instruction, like “Weather” or “Landforms”.
  5. Click the “+” icon to add to an existing collection or create a new collection.
  6. Select My Collections to review or share your collection.
  7. Share these books with your class or read together as a class.
