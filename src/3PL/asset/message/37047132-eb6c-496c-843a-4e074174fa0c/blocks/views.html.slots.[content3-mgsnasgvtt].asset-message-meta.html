Here’s how to get started:

  • From your Teacher Console, select ‘Assign & Review’ in the left
    navigation
    (TIP: if you do not see this option, you may still be in the Admin Console. Simply select ‘Teacher View’, the last option in the left navigation.)
  • Click ‘Refreshed Assign and Review’
  • Click on any Topic to see the Activities list drop down
  • Choose one or more Activities to assign by clicking the checkbox to the
    right of the Activity
