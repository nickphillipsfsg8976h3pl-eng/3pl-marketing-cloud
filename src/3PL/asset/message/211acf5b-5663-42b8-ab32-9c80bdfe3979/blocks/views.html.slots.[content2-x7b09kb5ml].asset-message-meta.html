1. Log in to your Reading Eggs Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click 'Assignments'
  3. Choose 'Create Assignments'
  4. Select the students you want to take the test
  5. Set the start and finish dates
  6. Opt for ‘Driving Test’ in the Type of Activity drop-down menu
  7. Select the 'Driving Test' that you are assigning
  8. Click Create this assignment. Once your students complete the Driving Test, you can effortlessly review their results in the ‘Manage Assignments’ section or in the Driving Tests Report (found in the Reports section).
 
⚡Once your students complete the Driving Test, you can effortlessly review their results in the ‘Manage Assignments’ section.
