1. Mental Minute

 

If you haven’t explored it with your class yet, Mental Minute is a powerful (and super fun!) maths fact fluency game that helps increase student confidence, skills and boost motivation with maths.

 

Why not assign Mental Minute and watch your students have so much fun racing the clock they won't even realise they are doing extra practise?
