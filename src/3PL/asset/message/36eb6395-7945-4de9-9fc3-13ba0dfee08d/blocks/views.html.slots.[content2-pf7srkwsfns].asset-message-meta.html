Why do we need student self-reflection?
 

All the pressure to catch up on the curriculum often has us falling back on summative assessments to pinpoint learning gaps. But smaller, formative assessments can be just as insightful.

 

In terms of bang for buck, it doesn't get much better than student self-reflections (when done right!). Not only does it help you see how students view their own progression, it pushes students to think more analytically about their own learning experiences, identify strengths and weaknesses in their knowledge and skillsets and refocus their goals.
