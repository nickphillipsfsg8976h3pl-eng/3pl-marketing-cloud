Engage minds, energise bodies: try multisensory learning


 

Harness your students’ energy by providing a whole-body learning experience with Reading Eggs’ multisensory activities. Showcase these ideas on your interactive whiteboard to get your students moving while supporting and extending their phonological awareness skills.
  • Syllables games like Clapping Syllables in the Printables Library
  • Play “I Spy The Sound”
  • Chant rhymes & songs
These phonological awareness activities are perfect for a whole class or small group. Try these with your students and get them ready for reading!
