Ready to integrate Mathletics into your daily classroom routine? 

At Calvary Christian School, teacher Elise Vargas shows what's possible: “Mathletics is used every time we do maths. From the teacher resources to the in-class lessons, you save time everywhere. It's one of those tools that you can jump into straight away." 

 Here are proven strategies to maximise learning during class time! 

🚀 Start your lesson strong: 
  • Warm-up with fluency activities to get brains engaged 
  • Demo with your student account to show concepts to the whole class. 
📊 During independent work: 
  • Group by ability levels to target exactly where students are 
  • Create Maths Stations and rotate through Mathletics activities 
  • Assign Skill Quests for structured learning journeys students love. 
🎯 For assessment & challenge: 
  • Quick diagnostic checks for instant understanding insights 
  • Problem Solving & Reasoning to challenge higher-order thinking 
  • Live Mathletics battles for real-time competitive engagement. 
📝 Offline support: 
  • Print practice pages for reinforcement and homework. 
🔦 For step-by-step guides on how to assign in class, log into your Mathletics account, click ‘On-boarding and SUpport’. Search for the topic you’re interested in and follow the walk-throughs.  

💡 Pro tips for success: 
  • Start with one strategy and gradually add more 
  • Use Live Mathletics for instant engagement 
  • Combine with printables for blended learning 
  • Track progress through real-time reporting.
