What is the Reading Eggs Book Week Competition?
 
  • 4 weeks of FREE access to Reading Eggs
  • Book Week Challenge #1 - Your class will work together to read a many Reading Eggs Library books as possible (the top 3 classes will win prizes!)
  • Book Week Challenge #2 - Your class (or individual students) will submit their best story to the Reading Eggs story factory (student votes will determine the prize winner!)
  • Receive a bonus Book Week Activities Pack
