1. Login to Mathseeds using the username previously sent to you
  2. In the left menu bar of your teacher console, click ‘Management’ and then ‘Manage Students’
  3. Click ‘Download a sample CSV’ and populate with your students’ details

  4. Save the CSV file to your desktop, then click the button ‘Upload a CSV’

  5. Click ‘Complete Import’
