Reading Eggs
 

Reading Eggs uses a blended phonics approach. It is a great foundational program, helping students to blend letters and sound, decode words and build their vocabulary.

 

Reading Eggs has 120 lessons and when students are finished with Reading Eggs, they can do Storylands which helps transition them to Reading Eggspress.
