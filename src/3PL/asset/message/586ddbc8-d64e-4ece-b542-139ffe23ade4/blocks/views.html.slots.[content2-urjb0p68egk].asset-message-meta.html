Fast Phonics
 

Fast Phonics is a systematic synthetic phonics program for students learning to read or to fill gaps.
 

There are 20 levels known as Peaks and each peak covers different letters and sounds. You can also find heaps of phonics training and printable resources in Fast Phonics, including lesson plans, sound mats, flashcards and more.
