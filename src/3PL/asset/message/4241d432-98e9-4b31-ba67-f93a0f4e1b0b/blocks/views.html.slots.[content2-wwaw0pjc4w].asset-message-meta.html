Session Overview:

Getting Started with Persuasive Assessment

Date: Tuesday, 28th May 2024

Time: 9am AWST

Location: Online

Duration: 3 hours & 15 minutes
 

This workshop is designed to empower educators in enhancing their assessment skills and assist schools in achieving greater consistency in grading. During the session, you will delve into:

  • A comprehensive breakdown of Brightpath exemplars and their developmental attributes.
  • In-depth analysis of student writing, considering both authorial choices and writing conventions.
  • Hands-on practice in marking and evaluating your assessments.
