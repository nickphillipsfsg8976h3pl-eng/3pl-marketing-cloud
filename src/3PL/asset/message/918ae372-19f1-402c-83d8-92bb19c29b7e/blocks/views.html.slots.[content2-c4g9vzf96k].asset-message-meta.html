There's nothing quite like seeing a student fall in love with reading and take off on their own!
 

Ready to spark that magic sooner? Here are three proven ways to fast-track independent reading success:
 

1. Start a Reading Journal
Encourage students to track every book they read in a special journal. They can write a short summary, draw their favourite scene or rate the book with stars or stickers – creativity is the limit!

 

2. Run a Read-a-thon
Make reading a fun, goal-oriented challenge! Encourage your students to read a certain number of books within a set time. Track their progress with a class leaderboard or a reading journal where they record the books they’ve completed.

 

3. Make spelling an adventure
Turn spelling lessons into something to look forward to! Set up a spelling scavenger hunt where students find hidden word cards or challenge them to create fun stories using their weekly spelling words.
