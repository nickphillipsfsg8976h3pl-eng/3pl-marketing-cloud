Effortlessly assign activities and quests: Your quick guide

Mathletics puts the power in your hands with a plethora of activities to enrich your students' learning.

First, here's how to assign a Mathletics Activity in Learn

  1. Log into your Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Go to 'Assign & Review' on the left-hand side
  3. Select 'Learn'
  4. Pick your 'Class' and 'Group
  5. Refine your search with the 'Topic Filter' button on the left
  6. Select the student and activity box
  7. Clik 'Assign'at the bottom
  8. Select the start and end date and click Assign.
  9. Explore our Knowledge Base for further guidance here: Assign an Activity in Mathletics


Ready to set your students on a math Skill Quest? It's a breeze! Here's how:

  1. Log into the Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Navigate to 'Assign & Review'
  3. Click 'Revise (Skill Quests)'
  4. Choose your 'Class' and 'Group'
  5. Select 'Curriculum' and 'Grade'
  6. Refine your search with the 'Filter' dropdown
  7. Pick the activity box for the student
  8. To assign to the group, select the box below the activity name
  9. Click 'Assign' at the bottom
  10. Keep an eye on the 'To-Do' tab under 'Quests' in the Student Console
  11. Explore our Knowledge Base for further guidance here: Assign a Quest in Mathletics
