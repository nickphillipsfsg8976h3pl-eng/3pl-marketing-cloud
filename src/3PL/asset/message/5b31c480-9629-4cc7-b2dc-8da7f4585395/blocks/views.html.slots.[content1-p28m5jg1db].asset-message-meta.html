Chances are, as an Australian teacher, you have heard about ABC Mathseeds - our multi-award-winning maths program that develops essential early maths skills and promotes a positive attitude towards maths. The great news is that Brightpath Maths, with its evidence-based assessments, seamlessly complements ABC Mathseeds! One program powers student growth while the other provides you with measurable proof of their progress.

 

How so? Every time students complete a Brightpath assessment, their score alongside this correlation chart enables you to position them precisely within ABC Mathseeds.

 

This also applies to Mathletics, developed to accommodate older students’ needs and boost their maths results in the most engaging way possible.
