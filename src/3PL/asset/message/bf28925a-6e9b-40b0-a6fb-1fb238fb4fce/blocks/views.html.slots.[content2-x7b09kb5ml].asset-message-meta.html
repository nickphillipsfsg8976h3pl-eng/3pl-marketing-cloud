Your students Mathseeds login cards are their golden ticket to hassle-free access and they’re easy to print, here’s how:

  1. Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click 'Management' on the left-hand side
  3. Select 'Manage Students/Pupils' from the list
  4. Check the box next to the students you want login cards for
  5. Hit the ‘Print Logins’ button
  6. A PDF document will pop up for you to download. Voilà!
