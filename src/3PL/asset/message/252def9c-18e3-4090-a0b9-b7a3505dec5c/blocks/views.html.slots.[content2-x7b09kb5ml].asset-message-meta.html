No Rollover Required

%%=v(@NAME)=%%

Yesterday we sent an email to your school regarding rollover for Brightpath. Your school does not need to perform Rollover, as it is handled by the WA SCSA (School Curriculum and Standards Authority).

In order to get rolled over for 2024, your school will only need to upload your SIRS and THREGS data to SCSA as per normal.

We apologise for any confusion this has caused. Please reach out to our support team at support@brightpath.com.au or 1300 850 331 if you have any questions or need any support regarding Brightpath Progress.

We wish you and your students a successful school year.

Best regards,
The Brightpath Support Team
