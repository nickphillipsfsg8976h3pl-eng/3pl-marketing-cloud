This week’s Quick Tip
🏎️ Start or end the day with a quick Mathletics challenge. 
 
Why this works?  
By assigning students an activity or running a round of Live Mathletics, tracking points and celebrating the winner, you create a fun, motivating routine that keeps everyone engaged.
