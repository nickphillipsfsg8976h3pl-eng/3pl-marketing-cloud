Ready to make maths time the most engaging part of your students' day? Here are strategies that spark genuine excitement:  

🚀 Quick engagement wins: 
  • Demonstrate concepts using Student View for whole-class instruction 
  • Use interactive tools in Rainforest Maths and Maths A-Z for dynamic learning 
  • Start with Times Tables Toons to kick off lessons with fun and energy. 

🎮 Master the Student Console: Explore Mathletics in Student View to see firsthand how your learners interact with the platform. 

💡 Pro tip: Access a Demo student account by signing into your teacher account, select Student View and choose your class and group.  

 🏅 Live Mathletics: This real-time maths game is pure engagement gold! Students challenge each other in 60-second races that test speed and accuracy of maths fluency. 

 Want to see what's inside each level? 
  1. Log in to your Mathletics Teacher Console 
  2. Click 'Student View', select the demo student
  3. Click 'Play', choose 'Live Mathletics' 
  4. Click on any level from 1 to 10 to unveil the questions! 
The results speak for themselves! At Al-Faisal Colleges, Head of College Faten Moussali has seen exactly what these strategies can achieve: 
"Engagement has really improved over the years, especially for students who used to find maths intimidating or boring. They're a lot more confident now and actually look forward to math time!"
