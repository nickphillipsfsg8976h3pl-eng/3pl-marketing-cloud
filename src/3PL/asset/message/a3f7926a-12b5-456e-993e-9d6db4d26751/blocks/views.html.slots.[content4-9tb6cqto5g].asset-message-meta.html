When students begin using Mathseeds they'll be prompted to take a Placement Test or start at Lesson 1. The Placement Test is fantastic for evaluating their current knowledge and essential for ensuring students start at the right point.

 

If you need to reset a placement test, simply follow these steps:

 

  • Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  • Click 'Management'
  • Select 'Manage Progress'
  • Hit 'Edit' next to the student's name
  • Under 'My Lessons' choose 'Placement Test'
  • Don't forget to click 'Save changes'
