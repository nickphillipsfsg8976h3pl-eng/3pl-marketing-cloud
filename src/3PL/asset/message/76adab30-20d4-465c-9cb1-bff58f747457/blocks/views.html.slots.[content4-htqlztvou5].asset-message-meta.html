Session 3: Industry Leaders share their experiences using Brightpath Progress Writing Assessment

Date: Monday, 18th September

Time: 4pm AEST
 

Guest Speakers: Suzie Stanger - Director of Literacy and Numeracy Program at SA Department of Education. Within the directorate is professional learning and coaching support for Brightpath Writing assessment tool, the Literacy Guarantee coaching support and professional learning and Progressive Assessment Testing to inform teacher practice.  

 

Deborah Hemming - Manager, Literacy and Numeracy Assessment and Improvement Team at SA Department of Education. She manages the Brightpath Writing team to support teachers and leaders in the use of the Brightpath Writing Assessment tool across South Australia.
