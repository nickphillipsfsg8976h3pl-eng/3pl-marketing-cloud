Session 1: Understanding development in writing

Date: Monday, 28th August

Time: 4pm AEST
 

In the first session, Dr. Sandy Heldsinger will illuminate the stages of student writing development. Each text type presents distinct demands on crafting sentences and paragraphs, and these differences can often be overlooked. By understanding the nuanced changes in writing development, educators can better equip themselves to teach writing effectively. At the conclusion of this session, you'll receive valuable resources to facilitate discussions with your staff, enabling them to refine their teaching and assessment practices.
