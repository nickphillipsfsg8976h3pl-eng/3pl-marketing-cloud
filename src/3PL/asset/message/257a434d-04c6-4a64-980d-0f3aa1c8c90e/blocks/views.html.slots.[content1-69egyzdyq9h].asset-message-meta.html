Enjoy with Your Mathletics Trial

Did you know your Mathletics trial gives you access to over 600 print-friendly worksheets in the Mathletics library? You do now!

These printable worksheets are a perfect way to compliment your child's screen time learning while you enjoy your Mathletics trial. Dive into practice questions, activities and exam revision. Oh, and don’t worry about having to check the answers for your children’s math work, the solutions are included!


The topics covered are aligned with the curriculum in your child's year level.

To access your free bonus resources and get started, follow these steps:
 

  • Log in to the Parent Dashboard 
  • Click Resources 
  • Filter by grade(s)  
  • Use the search bar to look for a specific topic  
  • Select an e-book  
  • Choose the student book and click Download.  

Note: You also have the option to download the teacher book for answers or assessments.
