Confirm your Mathletics Subscription


  1. Confirm your student numbers.

  2. Update your student details.
    Login to your Mathletics admin console and click on rollover and follow the prompts.

What is Rollover?



The Rollover process enables you to make necessary changes to last year's school roll, including adding or removing students and updating class information.

Completing Rollover ensures your teachers and students are assigned to the correct classes and grade levels as they start using Mathletics for the new academic year.

To complete the Rollover process, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Sign In to your Mathletics Administrator Account

  2. Follow the promots to download last year's school roll and update it with this year's student information

  3. Upload the updated school roll and save the changes



If you have any questions or need any assistance, please reach out to our dedicated Rollover Support Team at rollover@3plearning.com. We're here to help you every step of the way.

For immediate support:

Please visit the Mathletics Knowledge Base and search for Rollover in the search bar.

If you've recieved this email but are not the appropriate contact, click here to let us know.

Thank you for your attention to this crucial task to ensure a seamless start for all your students and teachers.
