Welcome to Term 1!

 

This term, all schools in NSW began teaching the new NSW Mathematics K–10 Syllabus 2023. It seems like a big change, so it’s good to know that ABC Mathseeds is already aligned with the new Maths syllabus and ready to go!

 

To help you use Mathseeds and Mathseeds Prime alongside the new syllabus, we’ve made correlation charts that show how the Lessons, Driving Tests, Mental Minute fluency program, and printable resources match neatly with the new syllabus.
