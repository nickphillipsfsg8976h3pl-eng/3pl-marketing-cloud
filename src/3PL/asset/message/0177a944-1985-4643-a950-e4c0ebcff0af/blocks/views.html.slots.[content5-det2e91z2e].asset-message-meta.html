Getting your child started!
 

To access the student console, head to the Mathletics login page here.

 

Tip: Your child’s login details can be found in the “Student Details” section in the Parent Hub.

 

Trick: Your child will quickly discover that they earn points for completing activities. If they get 1000 points, we’ll send them a special certificate in the mail. Put them to the challenge!
