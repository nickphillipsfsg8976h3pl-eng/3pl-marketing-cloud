Our research-based, structured maths approach with well-sequenced content is designed to be highly engaging, accessible, and to accelerate learning and confidence through:

 

✨ Utilising a structured maths approach with High-Impact Teaching Strategies and Explicit Teaching.
 

✨ Enabling students to systematically build their knowledge and skills with a well-sequenced learning pathway, each question set building knowledge and skills in a clear progression to achieve mastery of concepts.
 

✨ Well-structured question sets: Within each 10-question set, the structure is always the same:

  • 5 fluency questions, encourage students to recall new concepts and skills, and calculate efficiently
  • 3 reasoning questions, focus on analysing, proving, evaluating and explaining
  • 2 problem-solving questions, where students develop the ability to interpret, formulate, model and investigate problem situations

✨ Each question set is accompanied by a Concept Refresh Video, and every question has a hint and a worked solution, providing a structured, supportive and predictable learning experience.
