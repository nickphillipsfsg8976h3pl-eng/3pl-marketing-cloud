To review your students’ writing and provide feedback simply follow these steps:

  1. Log into your Writing Legends Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on 'Recent Writing’ from the Teacher Home screen
  3. Open 'View Writing' to review student's submissions
  4. Offer valuable feedback through comments, marks or stickers.
