Every student learns differently and Reading Eggs makes it easy to meet each one exactly where they are. 

At Kula Elementary, teacher Lisa Silva discovered exactly this: "The differentiation in Reading Eggs is something I really appreciate because students are all at different levels," she notes. "I love that the initial testing places students into what they know and where they need to go." 

Here's how you can achieve the same results in your classroom: 
🧠 Personalised pathways 
Placement tests ensure students start at the perfect level based on ability - not age. Lessons adjust based on responses, challenging advanced readers while supporting those needing more practice. 

📚 Comprehensive content and digital library 
Reading Eggs, Reading Eggspress and Fast Phonics target phonics to comprehension, plus 4000+ eBooks (including 350+ decodable readers) at varying levels match student ability and interests. 

⚖️ Perfect balance of independence & control 
Students explore games, books and activities at their own pace, while teachers maintain full control to assign specific lessons, books and assessments targeting individual learning goals. 

📊 Data-backed insights that drive action 
Real-time reporting reveals each student's strengths, gaps and growth, helping you group students effectively, assign targeted resources and provide the support each learner needs. 

🎯 Built-In motivation & mastery 
Golden eggs, certificates and immediate feedback keep students engaged while regular mastery checks ensure retention, automatically redirecting students to review when needed. 

👩‍🏫 Seamless intervention support 
Perfect for structured literacy interventions and targeted support programs with scaffolded lessons building foundational skills.
