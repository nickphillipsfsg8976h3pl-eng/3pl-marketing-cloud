You can connect maths to the real-world AND build soft skills all with Mathletics.
How?

Mathletics has helpful videos of mathematical concepts in action to go along with curriculum-based lessons and activities. Students love these videos and often tell us these videos deliver that light bulb ‘aha I get it!’ moment. Check out these videos on Maths in Cooking and Maths in Sport to see examples of how Mathletics makes math concepts real.

With over 700 problem solving and reasoning activities, it’s also easy to build those soft skills with Mathletics. Go beyond the formula and help students deepen their understanding of how to apply what they’ve learnt.
