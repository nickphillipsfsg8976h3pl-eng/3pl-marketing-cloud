How do the best teachers tackle PSR?
 

Our team of education specialists have boiled down great PSR lessons to a few simple principles:

 
  • Get prepared early
  • Hone your questions
  • Group the class to the activity (and not the other way around)
  • Think about your timing
  • Leave time to reflect
 

Want to know more? Read on to find out how you can take problem-solving & reasoning to a new level in your classroom.
