QUESTIONS? GET IN TOUCH WITH OUR SUPPORT TEAM
 
 




  1. Log into your Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Navigate to 'Assign and Review'
  3. Select 'Assess'
  4. Choose the 'Class' and 'Group'
  5. Under Curriculum/Activity Filters choose Curriculum, Grade/Year Level, and Assessment that you assigned
  6. Select the Assessment Type (Pre-Test, Post-Test, Snapshot Check, or Custom Test)
  7. Click the 'book' icon next to a student's name
  8. Hit 'Review Assessment'
  9. A new tab opens with the student's completed assessment for your review.
