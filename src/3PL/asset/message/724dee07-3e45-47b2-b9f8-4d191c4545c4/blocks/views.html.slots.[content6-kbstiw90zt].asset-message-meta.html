To access Writing Legends, set up your students through your ABC Mathseeds account. Here's how:
  1. Log in to your ABC Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on the ‘Writing’ tab located at the top left of the dashboard
  3. In the Navigation panel, click on 'Management' then 'Manage Students'
  4. Select 'Copy Classes' then click 'Copy'
  5. You and your students can enjoy free access until 31 October 2023.
