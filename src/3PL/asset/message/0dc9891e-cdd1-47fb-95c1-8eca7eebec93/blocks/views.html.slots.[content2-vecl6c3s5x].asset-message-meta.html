Getting started with learning groups:

 

Mathletics makes it easy to organise learning groups based on individual abilities - helping you deliver the right content to the right students.

 

Here's how:

  • Log in to your Mathletics Teacher Console
  • Click 'Manage Students'
  • Select 'Groups', then 'New Group'
  • Fill in the group information
  • Add students using the dropdown menu
