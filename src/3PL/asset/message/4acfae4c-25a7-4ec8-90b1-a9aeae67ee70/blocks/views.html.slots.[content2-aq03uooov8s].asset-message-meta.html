If your students are having trouble logging into the mobile app, their password may be too short (fewer than four characters). We have identified a few of these short passwords in your school. This issue only affects the app, not web logins.

To resolve this:

  • Update their passwords to be at least four characters long and print new login cards. Instructions found here.
  • In the meantime, they can still use their old QR logins – these will still work.
  • Alternatively, students can log in via the website instead of using the app: sso.readingeggs.com/login

If you need help, reach out to our customer service team at customerservice@3plearning.com, and we can reset passwords in bulk.
