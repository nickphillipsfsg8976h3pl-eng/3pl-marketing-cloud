1. Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on the ‘Reading’ tab located at the top left of the dashboard
  3. In the Navigation panel, click on ‘Management’ then ‘Manage Students’. Select 'Copy Classes'.
  4. Under ‘Copy classes from’ select ‘Math’ then select classes to copy and click ‘Copy selected classes’
  5. You and your students can now enjoy free access to Reading Eggs!
