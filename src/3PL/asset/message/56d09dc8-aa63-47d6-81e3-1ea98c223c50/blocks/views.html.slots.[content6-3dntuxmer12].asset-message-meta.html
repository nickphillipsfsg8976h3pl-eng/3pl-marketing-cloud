How to set up classes in Writing Legends?
  1. Log in to your ABC Reading Eggs and ABC Mathseeds account.
  2. Click the blue Writing tab in the top left of your Teacher Dashboard.
  3. Using the side navigation, select Management.
  4. From here, select the Copy Class button (on the right) and simply copy your existing classes into Writing Legends.
 
 

*Note: Your rollover must be completed before you can start a Writing Legends Trial. If you haven't done this yet, you can still contact our sales team to schedule a Writing Legends Demo.
