Shane Davis, Business Manager - LiteracyPlanet & Brightpath, reflects on the impact of their partnership: 

"LiteracyPlanet has proudly partnered with The Smith Family in Australia for many years, providing complimentary licenses to help build literacy skills among the children they support.

The Smith Family team works tirelessly to engage with families and tackle the effects of educational inequality caused by poverty. They manage the LiteracyPlanet accounts with great care, closely monitoring student progress to ensure no child falls through the cracks.

We're honoured to play a small part in making a meaningful difference to these children’s futures."
