What’s Workplace Giving? 

Each fiscal year, 3P selects a pool of employee-nominated charities to support – helping us broaden our impact across diverse causes while still keeping our efforts focused and meaningful. 

How does it work? 

3P-ers can donate to any registered charity of their choice – anywhere in the world – and 3P will match their donation up to $500 AUD. 

💡 Here’s the twist: while you can donate to any cause close to your heart, 3P’s matched funds are pooled and distributed evenly across our selected charity partners. It’s a powerful way to support what matters to you, while also contributing to shared impact. 

In the last two rounds, each of our chosen charities received around $1,000 – and this year, we’re aiming to beat that, and also highlight more non-monetary ways to contribute.  

Got a charity in mind? 

If there’s a cause close to your heart that you'd like considered for FY26, email any member of the P&C team with: 

  • a brief overview of what the charity does 

  • why you think it should be included 

The Senior Leadership Team will carefully review all nominations, with our new FY26 partners announced in August.
