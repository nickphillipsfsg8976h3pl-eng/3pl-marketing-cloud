After 15 years of emails and partnership, a first hello in person! 

Amanda Santamaria and Allison Ireland (pictured above) first connected back in 2010–11 when Edmentum (then Archipelago Learning) acquired US distribution rights for Reading Eggs. Allison’s team handled content localisation, and she and Amanda became key contacts, building a strong partnership almost entirely over email. Years later, they kept in contact and Amanda helped bring Allison across to 3P in 2024 - and now, as of April, they’ve finally met face-to-face!
