*Note for district-associated and standalone schools: Schools associated with a district will inherit their district's SAS reporting standards. Standalone schools not associated with a district will need to set their own SAS standards. The district coordinator (for district schools) or Reading Eggs subscription coordinator (for standalone schools) can set and edit the SAS. If a standalone school merges with a district, the reporting standard will change to match the district's settings.
