Printing login cards
 

1. Click Manage Students.
 

2. Select your class or indiviudal students.
 

3. Select your printing configuration. Here you can choose to include the QR code. Students can simply scan their code to login, making the process a little smoother.
 

4. Click Download Sign-in Cards.
