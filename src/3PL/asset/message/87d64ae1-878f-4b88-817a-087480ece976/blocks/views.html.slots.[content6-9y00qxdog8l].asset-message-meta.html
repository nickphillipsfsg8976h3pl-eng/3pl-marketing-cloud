3 World Maths Day preparation tips that work

 

1. Make sure you have a login

Ensure your students are correctly allocated to their classes. Check out our helpful articles below for detailed instructions:
 

✅ How do I add a student to Mathletics?

✅ How do I add students to my class?

 

2. Use Live Mathletics to practice

Live Mathletics is the competitive engine behind World Maths Day. Your students can access it at any given time of day (or night) and find a peer somewhere in the world who is ready to pit their mathematics skills against theirs.
 

3. Trust in your fingers!

Have your students practise using Live Mathletics without looking at the keyboard – this skill comes in handy for rapid-fire typing!
