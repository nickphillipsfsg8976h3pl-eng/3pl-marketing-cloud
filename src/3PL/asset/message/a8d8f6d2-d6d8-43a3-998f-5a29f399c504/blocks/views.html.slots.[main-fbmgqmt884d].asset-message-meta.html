Let's explore how to make the most of our powerful writing program. This series of quick wins will make it as easy as possible for you – starting from the very beginning:  

🖨️ Print your student login cards to get started 

  1. Log in to your 3 Essentials Teacher Dashboard.  

  2. Click New Dashboard Support in the top right. 

  3. Select Print log-in cards for students from the list.  

  4. Follow the prompts as they appear on the screen. 

 
📚 Encourage student engagement 
Quick tip: Start with student placement. 
This can be done via assignable Placement Test, ensuring all your students are exactly where they need to be in the program for the best results. 
  1. Log in to your 3 Essentials Teacher Dashboard.  
  2. Click the green Writing tile.  
  3. Click Support in the top right and search for Assign students a Placement Test. 
  4. Follow the prompts as they appear on the screen. 
You can always reassign the Placement Test by following the same instructions. Once completed, the Placement Test provides teachers with actionable feedback in the Recent Writing area.
