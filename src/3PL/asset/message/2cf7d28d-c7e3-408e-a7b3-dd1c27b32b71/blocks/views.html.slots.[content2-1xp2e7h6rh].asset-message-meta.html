Use Mathletics to engage your students with thousands of questions to build strong foundational skills. The Understanding, Practice, Fluency (UPF) activities utilise rolling mastery (real-time ongoing, formative assessment of student proficiency) in order to offer your students the optimal next step in their learning journey.

 

UPF activities can be assigned to individual students to support personalised learning. Access this guide to learn more about previewing and assigning UPF activities.
