Now that your learning groups are set up within Mathletics, it’s time to kick off your students’ learning experience! Getting your students started is quick and easy.
 

Start by distributing your students’ usernames and passwords, then encourage them to log in and personalise their avatar. Next, a great area for your students to start exploring is Live Mathletics where they get to play 60-second fluency games against other students from their class, school, or around the world!
 

Personalise your students' learning
 

With your students now set up in groups, you can easily personalise their learning by assigning specific curriculum activities at a class, group, or student level. By personalising student activities you can ensure they will stay engaged and motivated as they learn. To set a learning course that challenges each of your students:
 
Click 'Teacher View'
Click 'Assign and Review'
Select 'Courses'
