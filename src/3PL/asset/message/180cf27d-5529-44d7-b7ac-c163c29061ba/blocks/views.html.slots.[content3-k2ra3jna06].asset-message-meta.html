It's time for the Mathletics Rollover, a crucial process which allows you to update your school's roster to reflect the new academic year.

What is Rollover?

The Rollover process enables you to make necessary changes to last year's school roster, including adding or removing students and updating class information. Completing Rollover ensures your teachers and students are assigned to the correct classes and grade levels as they start using Mathletics for the new academic year.

Ensuring your Rollover is completed by October will guarantee your teachers and students are correctly assigned to their respective classes and grade levels when they resume using Mathletics.

To complete the Rollover process, please follow these simple steps:

  1. Sign in to your Mathletics Administrator Account
  2. Follow the prompts to download last year's school roll and update it with this year's student information
  3. Upload the updated school roll and save the changes

If you require additional support, we've created helpful videos and guides in the Mathletics Knowledge Base.
