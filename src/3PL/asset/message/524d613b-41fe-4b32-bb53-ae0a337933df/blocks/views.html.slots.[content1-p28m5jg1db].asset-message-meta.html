What if you could see exactly where each student stands and know precisely what to teach next? That's the power of Brightpath Progress.
 

Let us share a remarkable story from Ballarat Clarendon College, where they transformed their English curriculum from Kindergarten to Year 12.
 

In 2017, despite satisfactory NAPLAN results, the college embarked on a journey to improve their students' writing performance. By 2022, their commitment to improvement had led to significant gains in their NAPLAN results.
 

The secret to their success? A carefully structured approach combining explicit teaching with strategic assessment.
