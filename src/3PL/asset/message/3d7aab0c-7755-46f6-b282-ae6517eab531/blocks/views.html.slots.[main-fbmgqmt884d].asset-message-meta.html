At one Reading Eggs school, a teacher explains how Reading Eggs has improved student outcomes: "Our children are no longer afraid of reading. They now love to read and because they're reading more, their results have improved." 

Here's how you can do the same! 

📚 Start with Placement Tests 

Before starting, have your students complete their Placement Test to: 
  • Show you exactly where each student needs support 
  • Eliminate guesswork about readiness 
  • Save you from re-teaching concepts students already understand. 
Need to reset a Placement Test? If a student needs to retake their placement test, you can easily reset it from your dashboard. Simply log in to your Teacher Dashboard, click ‘Management’, then select ‘Manage Progress’. Click ‘Edit’ next to the student's name, choose ‘Placement Test’ under ‘My Lessons’, and don't forget to click ‘Save changes’ to give them a fresh start. 

🎯 Build the weekly habit: 
  • Encourage your students to log in at least 3 times per week 
  • Challenge them to earn 1000 points through their Weekly Quest 
  • Celebrate certificates to maintain momentum! 
Teachers like Victoria find that: "Pupils are willing to tackle harder activities because they can see the progression they're making and they enjoy interacting with the program." 

🔧 Make the most of your dashboard with these time-saving tools: 
  • Printables Library: Grab curriculum-aligned worksheets instantly 
  • Real-time reports: See exactly who needs help at a glance. 
Many schools combine Mathseeds with Reading Eggs for students who need foundational support across both literacy and numeracy. Explore Mathseeds for your classroom by trialling through your 3P Dashboard.
