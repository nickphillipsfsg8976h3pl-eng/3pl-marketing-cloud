✅ First, students record themselves reading for one minute, with the flexibility of three attempts to ensure confidence and accuracy.

 

✅ Next, teachers dive in: listen, assign a mark and add personalized comments. These invaluable insights serve as clear evidence of progress, offering a window into students’ journey towards reading mastery.

 

✅ Plus, easily share comparisons between early and recent readings with parents, fostering transparent communication and collaboration.

 

✅ Dive deeper with comprehensive reports showcasing improvements over time. These reports, tailored to Lexile standards, provide a holistic view of each student’s growth.
