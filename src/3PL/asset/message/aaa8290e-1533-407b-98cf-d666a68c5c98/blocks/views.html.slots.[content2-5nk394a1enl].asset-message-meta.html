Positive behaviour is simpler with digital resources
 

Maintaining a positive learning environment for students is important, but how do we do this without pulling focus away from quality learning?

 

Digital resources with engaging content, fun challenges, and ongoing rewards (such as points, games, certificates, and more!), are designed to support classrooms and provide the positive reinforcement needed to show students their effort is noticed and valued.

 

Using online learning time as a reward for good behaviour and effort is also a great motivator for even the most reluctant of learners.
