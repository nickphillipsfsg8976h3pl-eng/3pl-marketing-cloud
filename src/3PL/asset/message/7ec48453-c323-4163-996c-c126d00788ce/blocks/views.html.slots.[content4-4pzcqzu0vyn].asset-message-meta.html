It’s easy to review assessment results here’s how:

Step 1: Log into Mathletics Teacher Console
Step 2: Navigate to 'Assign & Review'
Step 3: Click 'Assess'
Step 4: Pick your ‘Class’ and ‘Group’
Step 5: Under Curriculum/Activity Filters choose Curriculum, Grade/Year Level, and Assessment that you assigned
Step 6: Select the Assessment Type (Pre-Test, Post-Test, Snapshot Check, or Custom Test)
Step 7: Click the 'book' icon next to a student's name
Step 8: Hit 'Review Assessment'
Step 9: A new tab opens with the student's completed assessment for your review.

With Mathletics, assessment becomes a breeze and your students’ progress is your insight, to tailor your teaching and create ability groups within your class.
