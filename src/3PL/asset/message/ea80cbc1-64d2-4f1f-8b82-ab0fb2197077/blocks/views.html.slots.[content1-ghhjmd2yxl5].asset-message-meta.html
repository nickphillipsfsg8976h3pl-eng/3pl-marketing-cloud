The Mathletics data reset will occur on July 29 for this school year. What does this mean?

  • Results of Activities (Activity Mastery activities), Skill Quests (formerly Understanding, Practice & Fluency tasks), and Challenges (Problem-Solving & Reasoning tasks) in the ‘To do’ area, and progress bars are all reset in student accounts.
  • Any assigned tasks or activities are reset.
  • Any certificates, points and MCoins in the student console will also reset at this time
  • Certificate achievement levels will also be reset so that students start from Bronze. Certificates awarded during the 2023-2024 school year will still be accessible in the Teacher and Student Centres.

We suggest downloading the Admin Activity & Usage Report, as it is the most comprehensive. Downloading these records is crucial to avoid missing any participation and activity data from your students.

To notify the student’s parents or guardians about the data reset, use this printable parent letter.
