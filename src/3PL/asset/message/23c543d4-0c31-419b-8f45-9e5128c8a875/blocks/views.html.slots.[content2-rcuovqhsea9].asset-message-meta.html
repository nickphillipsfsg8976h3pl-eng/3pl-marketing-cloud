Assignments Guide
 

In Assignments you can assign a lesson or Driving Test to an individual student or your class.

 

You can set a time frame. Students will be locked into the activity until they have completed it, the due date has passed, or you have expired the assignment.
 

See this Assignments Guide to find out everything you need to know.
