In a recent report published by The Progress in International Reading Literacy Study (PIRLS), it was concerning to learn that in 2021, 81% of South African Grade 4 learners were unable to read for meaning in any language, a worsening figure compared to 2016.

 

These findings reinforce the profound significance of elevating reading levels, due to the vital role they play in shaping comprehension, understanding and overall cognitive development.
