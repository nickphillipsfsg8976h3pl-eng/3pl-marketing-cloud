How students can explore writing prompts and harness the power of instant feedback:
  1. From the Student Home Screen, select 'Exciting Writing Ideas'
  2. Explore prompts, filter by type or view ongoing tasks 
  3. Choose a prompt, listen to the introduction, and refer to the sample 
  4. Write, review against a checklist and hit the target word count 
  5. Experience the game-changing Instant Legend Feedback, allowing for revisions before teacher evaluation! 
  6. Access teacher feedback by selecting 'My Portfolio' on the Student Home screen.
