End of lesson Skills Quiz
 

When students complete a lesson, they will now also sit a quick 10 question, multiple choice Skills Quiz!

 

These are quick, low-stress assessments and will help you quickly identify who may need some intervention or re-teaching. Look out for the Skills Masters tile in your reporting area!
