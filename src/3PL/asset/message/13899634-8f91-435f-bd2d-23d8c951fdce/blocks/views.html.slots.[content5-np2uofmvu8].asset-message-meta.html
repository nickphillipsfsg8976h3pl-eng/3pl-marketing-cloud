NAPLAN practice exams are available in Mathletics!
 

Did you know you can administer a NAPLAN practice exam in your math class? Follow these five simple steps to get started today:
  1. Sign in to the Mathletics Teacher console.
  2. Click 'Assign and Review'.
  3. Click 'Assessments'.
  4. Click 'Curriculum/Activity Filters'.
  5. Select the grade and assessment required.
