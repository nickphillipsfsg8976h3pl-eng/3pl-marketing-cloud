Explore your Teacher Toolkit: a goldmine of engaging
letter-sound activities

Build phonics skills in your classroom by using engaging activities designed to focus on individual letter sounds. You can try these ideas with the whole class to cover grade-level standards.

Additionally, using them in small-group sessions lets students practice their social skills during their literacy journey. Every Peak has a matching Teaching Guide and Slide Deck to support in-class teaching.
