Webinar Sessions:

 

Session 1: Understanding development in writing

Date: Monday, 28th August

Time: 4pm AEST
 

In the first session, Dr. Sandy Heldsinger will illuminate the stages of student writing development. Each text type presents distinct demands on crafting sentences and paragraphs, and these differences can often be overlooked. By understanding the nuanced changes in writing development, educators can better equip themselves to teach writing effectively. At the conclusion of this session, you'll receive valuable resources to facilitate discussions with your staff, enabling them to refine their teaching and assessment practices.

 

Presenter: Dr Sandy Heldsinger, Brightpath Progress Founder & Assessment Expert

Dr. Sandy Heldsinger, a trailblazer in education assessment, has led WA's system-level assessments and taught master's level courses in the field. Her resource development, including innovative reporting software Brightpath Progress, empowers schools to optimise assessment for student success. Sandy's influence extends to curriculum shaping, notably co-authoring "What Teachers Need to Know About Assessment and Reporting." Her dedication earned her the WAPPA Service Award in 2016 for her significant impact on education.
 

Co-Presenter: Mark Scruby, Head of English P-12 at Ballarat Clarendon College, VIC

With over a decade of experience, Mark has led curriculum reforms focused on explicit instruction in writing, spelling, and grammatical knowledge. He will share how Brightpath Progress has been instrumental in developing effective assessment strategies to gauge curriculum efficacy and student progress.
