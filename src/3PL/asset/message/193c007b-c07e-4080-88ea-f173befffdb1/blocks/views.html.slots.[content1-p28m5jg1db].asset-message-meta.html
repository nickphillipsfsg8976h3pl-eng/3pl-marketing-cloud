Unlocking Effective Writing Assessment: Empowering Teachers for Success.
 

%%=v(@NAME)=%%
 
 

Here’s your exclusive invitation for a transformative three-part webinar series focused on enhancing your understanding of effective writing assessment and equipping you with strategies to support your teachers in their journey to assess students' work confidently.
 

Webinar Series Overview:

 

Session 1: Monday, 28th August 4pm AEST

Session 2: Monday, 11th September 4pm AEST

Session 3: Monday, 18th September 4pm AEST
 

For the past two decades, educators have recognised the pivotal role of assessment in effective teaching. Yet, the challenges of assessing and reporting student achievements, especially in writing, persist. Writing assessment demands a nuanced approach that goes beyond the realm of multiple-choice questions and automated scoring. Teachers need to grasp the intricacies of writing conventions and understand how to analyse the strengths and weaknesses of student compositions.

 

In this dynamic series, we will:

  • Explore strategies for effective writing assessment
  • Gain insights into supporting your teachers in assessing student work.
  • Understand the stages of student writing development.
  • Learn about the nuanced changes in writing development across different text types.
  • Uncover resources to enhance teaching and assessment of writing.
 

Dr. Sandy Heldsinger, a distinguished researcher in writing assessment and founder of Brigthpath Progress, a formative assessment online program, will lead the discussion, sharing her insights and valuable tips to set your teachers on the path to success.
