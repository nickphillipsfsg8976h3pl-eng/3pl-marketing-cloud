Step 4: Explore the Student Centre

 

Student Centre is the first thing your students see when they log in to Mathletics – it’s their personalised homepage, designed to keep them motivated and on track. Getting familiar with it can make all the difference on World Maths Day!

 

⭐a dynamic student dashboard showing their achievements and progress

⭐ three clear learning zones — Learn, Revise and Assess

⭐ weekly challenges with surprise rewards

⭐ points tracking that celebrates every step forward.
 

Take a few minutes to explore the Student Centre yourself — seeing it through your students' eyes is the best way to understand how it will work in your classroom.
