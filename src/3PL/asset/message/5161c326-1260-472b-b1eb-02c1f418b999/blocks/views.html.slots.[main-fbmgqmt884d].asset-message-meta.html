At one Mathseeds school, a teacher shares: 
“I like that the students are placed according to what they already know. I like that the program teaches multiple strategies and allows students to have a deeper, more concrete understanding of numbers and concepts.” 

Here's how you can do the same! 

📚 Start with Placement Tests 
Before starting, have your students complete their Placement Test to: 
  • Show you exactly where each student needs support 
  • Eliminate guesswork about readiness 
  • Save you from re-teaching concepts students already understand. 
🎯 Build the weekly habit: 
  • Encourage your students to log in at least 3 times per week 
  • Challenge them to earn 1000 points through their Weekly Quest 
  • Celebrate with certificates to maintain momentum! 
🔧 Make the most of your dashboard with these time-saving tools: 
  • Printables Library: Grab curriculum-aligned worksheets instantly 
  • Real-time reports: See exactly who needs help at a glance. 
Many schools combine Reading Eggs with Mathseeds for students who need foundational support across both literacy and numeracy. Explore Reading Eggs for your classroom by trialling through your 3 Essentials Dashboard.
