Streamline assessment with Mathletics: Your simple guide

Assessing your students' progress to refine your teaching strategies has never been easier with Mathletics.

To assign assessments simply follow these steps:

Step 1: Log into Mathletics Teacher Console
Step 2: Click 'Assign & Review'
Step 3: Select 'Assess'
Step 4: Choose the 'Class' and 'Group'
Step 5: Filter your desired assessment under Curriculum/Activity Filters
Step 6: Select the Curriculum and Grade/Year
Step 7: Choose the Assessment
Step 8: Click 'Assign' at the bottom.

⚡Pro Tips:

  • Refine your assessment by filtering topics and questions
  • Swiftly assign to all students with the 'lightning bolt' icon or individually select students
  • Set assessment or question lengths to optimize time
  • Randomize questions to deter copying
  • Schedule assessments for later or assign now for instant engagement
