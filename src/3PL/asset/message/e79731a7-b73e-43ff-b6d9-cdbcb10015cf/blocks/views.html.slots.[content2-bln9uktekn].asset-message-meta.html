What is the Reading Eggspedition?

  • 2-week online literacy event.
  • Free access to Reading Eggs (plus Fast Phonics, Reading Eggspress and the Library!).
  • Weekly prizes up for grabs.
  • Super engaging and fun for students.
  • Heaps of resources for teachers.
 
 
 

What is the Reading Eggspedition?

 

If you're having a tough time engaging students with reading or just need to inject some enthusiasm and energy into your classroom, the Reading Eggspedition is perfect. With different goals each week, your students will love competing to spend more and more time working on their literacy skills.
