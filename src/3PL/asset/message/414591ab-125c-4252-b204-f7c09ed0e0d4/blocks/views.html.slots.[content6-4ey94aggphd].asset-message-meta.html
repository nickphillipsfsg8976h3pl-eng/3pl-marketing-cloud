Reading Eggs' parent letters are a way to involve parents in their child's progress and foster extra practice at home. Follow these quick steps to share Parent Letters:
  1. Log in to your Reading Eggs Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on 'Management'
  3. Click on 'Manage Students'
  4. Select 'All' students
  5. Click on 'Download parent letters and select the language
