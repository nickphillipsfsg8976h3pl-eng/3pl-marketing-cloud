• Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  • Click on 'Reports'
  • Select 'Dashboard'
  • Toggle to 'Table' at the top of the screen to drill down to student results
  • Click on the student's name
  • Pick the year/grade level and corresponding lessons
  • Click 'Export' to print a full report
