1. Log into your Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Navigate to 'Assign and Review'
  3. Select 'Revise (Skill Quests)'
  4. Pick your 'Class' and 'Group'
  5. Refine your search with the ' Filter' dropdown
  6. Select the student and activity box, or the group box below the activity namex
  7. Click 'Assign' at the bottom
  8. Keep an eye on the 'To-Do' tab under 'Quests' in the Student Console.
