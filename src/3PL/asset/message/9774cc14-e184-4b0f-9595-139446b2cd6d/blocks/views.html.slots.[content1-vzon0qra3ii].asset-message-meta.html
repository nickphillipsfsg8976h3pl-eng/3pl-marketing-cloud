One-size-fits-all may be true when buying leggings, but not in education.

 

To cater to young learners’ needs it’s rather one-size-fits-none.

 

The more individualised approach to learning we take, the higher students’ achievements are. Differentiated learning maximises student engagement by tailoring instruction to their individual strengths, interests, and learning styles. It is proven to boost student engagement and motivation.
