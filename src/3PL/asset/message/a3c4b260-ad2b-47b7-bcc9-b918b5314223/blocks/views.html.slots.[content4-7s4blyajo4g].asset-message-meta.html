How Reading Eggs supports you

 

Used and trusted in hundreds of New Zealand schools, our comprehensive program:
  • Supports a structured literacy approach to build the foundational reading skills of phonemic awareness, phonics and handwriting (for Phase 1).
  • Provides explicit instruction followed by scaffolded activities, independent practice and instant feedback to consolidate learning and build automaticity.
  • Uses a systematic scope and sequence designed to be cumulative providing spaced practice and effectively enabling skill building.
  • Offers access to teacher-developed ready-to-use resources with hundreds of phonetically decodable books.
  • Includes tools to monitor real-time student data to ensure you can always track progress.
  • Fosters a love for reading through its extensive digital library of over 4,000 titles and Reading Journal.
