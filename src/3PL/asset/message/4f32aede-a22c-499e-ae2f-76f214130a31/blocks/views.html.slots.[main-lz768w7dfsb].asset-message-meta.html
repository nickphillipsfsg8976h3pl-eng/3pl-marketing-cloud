🎯 Personalised Learning Pathways 

Choice of Writing Prompts: Students can select from a wide range of genres, themes and topics that suit their interests and ability levels. 

Adaptive Scaffolding: Built-in writing frameworks, model texts, and sentence starters help struggling writers, while more confident students can write with minimal support. 

🧠 Supports a Range of Abilities 
Remediation Support 

Step-by-step guidance, vocabulary suggestions and grammar prompts for learners who need extra help building writing confidence. 

Editable teacher feedback allows for targeted skill correction.

Extension Opportunities 
Open-ended prompts and creative challenges encourage advanced writers to experiment with style, tone and structure. 

Students can produce extended texts or tackle complex writing goals beyond their year level. 

📊 Real-Time Teacher Insights 

Student Progress Dashboard: Shows how each learner is performing, where they’re excelling, and which areas need improvement. 

Custom Assignments: Teachers can assign differentiated tasks to individuals, groups or the whole class. 


✏️ Engaging and Motivational Features 

Gamified Elements: Students earn badges and points for completing tasks. 

Student Voice and Choice: Opportunities to write creatively on topics they care about keep motivation high.
