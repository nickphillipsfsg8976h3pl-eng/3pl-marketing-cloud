My Lessons is a perfect place to start your students’ writing journey. It houses a collection of fully integrated, curriculum-aligned units, presented as thematic interactive maps that blend teaching strategies and independent practice seamlessly, focusing on one skill at a time.

Exciting Writing Ideas is where Writing Legends takes your students’ writing from good to great. To get their creative juices flowing, students can choose from Imaginative, Informative or Persuasive writing. From there, they’re guided with captivating visuals, valuable writing tips, a target word count to aim for and an audio discussion.

Instant Legends Feedback is where students receive real-time, personalised feedback on how to edit and improve their writing. We equipped Writing Legends with an extensive collection of over 650 writing prompts, tailored to various year levels and text types.

Realms is a world-building reward area that students can unlock and explore once they reach point milestones by engaging with Writing Legends.

#Books is an interactive, student-led way to expand vocabulary and broaden young readers’ horizons.
