This session we welcomed Allison Ireland, Director of Product with 3P Learning, as she presented on integrating the Science of Reading for vocabulary success.

We Discussed:
1. The Importance of Vocabulary in Early Literacy
2. The Imapct of COVID on Early Literacy
3. The Science of Reading and Vocabulary
4. Incorporating Vocabulary into the Classroom
5. Applying Additional Literacy Ideas

Allison explored how the Science of Reading isn't about teaching words, rather it's about equipping learners with the skills they need to understand, discern, and determine word meaning.

As educators, Allison's presentation equips us with several ways to use the Science of Reading to supercharge vocabulary learning in our classrooms.
