Confirmed: Mathletics Leads to Higher NAPLAN Results

We’ve never been more excited about research, especially with what it means for educators like you. 

A fresh comparative study confirms that there is a direct link between the use of Mathletics and improved student performance on national numeracy assessments.
The study revealed clear gains for both Year 3 and Year 5, with schools showing significant improvements when students used Mathletics just 15-30 minutes a day. 

What’s more, increasing usage led to an additional boost in scores!
