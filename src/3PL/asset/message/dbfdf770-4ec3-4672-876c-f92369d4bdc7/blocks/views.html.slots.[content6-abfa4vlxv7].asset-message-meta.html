A 3-week Reading Eggs trial for 59 pupils from King Fahad Academy resulted in increased engagement and completion of over 1000 learning activities!

 

When students were asked to choose which reading software they preferred, 87% chose Reading Eggs over other resources.

 

“It is engaging and easy to navigate. Children have been eagerly reading independently. They are keen to progress and do more at home.”
