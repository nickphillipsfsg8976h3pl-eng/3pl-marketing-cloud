Let us count all the ways...
 

...differentiating with EdTech can help save you time:
 
  1. Assign tasks to individuals or groups: One of the hallmarks of EdTEch is that you can easily assign activities, tasks and assessments for your students by class, group or individual - allowing you to drill down and differentiate on whatever level you need to.
  2. Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD): Some EdTech programs have sophisticated algorithms that will show your students content that is in their ZPD, making sure that they are always working at that 'not too hard, not too easy' level - maintaining progress and confidence.
  3. Data driven reporting: In order to differentiate you need to have the latest data at your fingertips. Digital programs allow you see exactly where your students' strengths and weaknesses are from their most recent activity and trends, so you can make differentiation decisions that much easier.
