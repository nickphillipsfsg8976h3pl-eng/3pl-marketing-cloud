Your student sign-in cards
 

Students tend to lose and forget things - you might have noticed that already. But lessons are at the mercy of the clock, and you've got to get your lessons started ASAP.

 

That's why we made student sign-in cards - to help you get the most out of every second of your class time.

 

These cards include students’ usernames and passwords as well as a QR code. Students can use these cards to login on a desktop or laptop.

 
 
 
  1. Click Manage Students
  2. Select the students or group
  3. Click 'Download sign-in cards'
  4. Choose the configuration
  5. Click Download

Your student sign-in cards
 

Students tend to lose and forget things - you might have noticed that already. But lessons are at the mercy of the clock, and you've got to get your lessons started ASAP.

 

That's why we made student sign-in cards - to help you get the most out of every second of your class time.

 

These cards include students’ usernames and passwords as well as a QR code. Students can use these cards to login on a desktop or laptop, or they can download the Mathletics App to sign in on their tablet.

 
  1. Click classes
  2. Click sign-in cards
  3. Select your class or group
  4. Click download
