Getting started with Reading Eggs: Once logged in, students are ready to go.

Make sure to explore 'Onboarding' and 'Support' to access helpful resources that will help you make the most of Reading Eggs in your classroom.

  1. Log in to your 3 Essentials Teacher Dashboard 
  2. Click ‘Support’, search for ‘Login cards’ 
  3. Follow the prompts
