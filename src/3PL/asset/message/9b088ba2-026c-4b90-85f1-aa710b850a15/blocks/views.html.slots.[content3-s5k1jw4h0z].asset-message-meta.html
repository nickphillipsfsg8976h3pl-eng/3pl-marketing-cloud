Getting started with lessons is simple for students – here’s how:

  1. Students start by selecting their unique avatar.
  2. Return to Student Home Screen with a tap.
  3. Select 'My Lessons'.
  4. Dive into 'Map 1' and kickstart with Lesson 1.
