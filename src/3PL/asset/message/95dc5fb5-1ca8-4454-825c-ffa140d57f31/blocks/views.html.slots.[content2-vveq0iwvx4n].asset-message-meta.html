Session Overview:

Brightpath Progress: Response to Literature - Assessment Launch

Date: Monday, 27th November

Time: 4pm AWST

Location: Online
 

During the webinar, Dr. Sandy Heldsinger will delve into the extensive research conducted to create this assessment. She will provide insights into the assessment itself and showcase calibrated exemplars. This session will also cover how this assessment can seamlessly integrate into your school's teaching and assessment program, aligning with the English curriculum, including any potential modifications to the P-10 curriculum. Additionally, you'll have the opportunity to gather evidence related to reading and writing skills.
 

This event offers teachers a chance to gain a comprehensive understanding of the new Brightpath "Response to Literature" assessment, allowing them to see its potential in their classrooms.
