Tips on how to make the most of your Mathletics trial and keep your child motivated with exciting rewards

 

%%=v(@NAME)=%%

To help you and your child get started with your Mathletics journey, we thought we’d send you some tips and tricks that will help you to navigate the program!

Get started by checking out the Parent Portal where you will find a selection of useful resources, including a welcome video designed to act as a tour guide for your Mathletics experience.

The Parent Portal also includes a Parent Pack filled with handy hints to help you maximise engagement with your little one at home.

Plus, have you discovered the “My Awards” section yet? Here you can celebrate your child’s hard work and track their progress with personalised Certificates and Achievement Levels.

Achievement levels show how often your child has earned 1000 points per week. These start at Bronze and progress through Silver, Gold, Platinum, Emerald, Diamond, Epic and Legend. Aim high and watch your child’s Achievement Levels Soar!
