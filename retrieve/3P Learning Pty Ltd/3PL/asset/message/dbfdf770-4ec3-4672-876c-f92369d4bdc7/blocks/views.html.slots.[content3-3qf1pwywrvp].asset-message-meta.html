• Engaging content: The program features a range of engaging content, including interactive games, animations, and songs, which helps keep students motivated and interested in learning.
  • Personalised learning: Reading Eggs is an adaptive program that automatically adjusts to each student's individual reading level, ensuring they always receive the appropriate level of instruction and challenge.
  • Explicit instructions: It provides students with guidance to develop skills across the five components of reading success: phonics, phonemic awareness, fluency, comprehension, and vocabulary.
  • Teacher support: The program comes with a range of teaching resources, including lesson plans and printable activities, to support classroom instruction.
  • Unlimited Accessibility: Reading Eggs can be used anywhere, anytime, on any device, both in the classroom or at home for individual reinforcement.
  • Developing a love of reading: with unrestricted access to a Library of 3,500+ eBooks to explore, and a Reading Journal where every reading achievement is celebrated, students are invited to delve into literature independently and foster a habit of reading for pleasure.
