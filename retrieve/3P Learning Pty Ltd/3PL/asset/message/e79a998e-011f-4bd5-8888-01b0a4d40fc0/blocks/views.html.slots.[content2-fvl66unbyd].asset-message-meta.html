Motivate with Meritopia!

 

Meritopia is Mathletics' inbuilt rewards system - designed to support learning outcomes and student engagement by rewarding continuous effort. Students have to complete activites to earn enough points to unlock Meritopia for 5 minute bursts.

 

Students adore Meritopia's funny surprises and can't wait to dive back into activities so they can earn more points. Watch this video to see how it works in action or start a trial today to experience it for yourself.
