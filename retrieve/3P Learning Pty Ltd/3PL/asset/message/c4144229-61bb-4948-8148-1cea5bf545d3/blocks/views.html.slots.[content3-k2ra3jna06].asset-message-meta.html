The Reading Success Triangle: Skill, Will and Thrill

As teachers, we know that developing reading skills is just part of the journey. We also want to create an environment where students actively choose to read and discover genuine enjoyment in reading.

  • Skill (Ability to Read): Systematic, explicit instruction and progressive skill building embedded in our programs help students read proficiently. Starting with foundational skills like phonics, they advance to fluency, comprehension and vocabulary. As they read more, they encounter diverse sentence structures, new vocabulary and genres, further enhancing proficiency.
  • Will (Motivation to Read): Rewards like a personalised Reading Journal spark intrinsic motivation, encouraging students to read for pleasure. When reading is enjoyable, children naturally seek more opportunities to explore it.
  • Thrill (Enjoyment of Reading): With the skills and motivation to read, students discover the true thrill of reading. Access to over 4,000 diverse texts in the Reading Eggspress library ensures thereâ€™s something to excite every reader and make reading an adventure.

By combining Skill, Will and Thrill, Reading Eggs creates a pathway to lifelong reading success.
