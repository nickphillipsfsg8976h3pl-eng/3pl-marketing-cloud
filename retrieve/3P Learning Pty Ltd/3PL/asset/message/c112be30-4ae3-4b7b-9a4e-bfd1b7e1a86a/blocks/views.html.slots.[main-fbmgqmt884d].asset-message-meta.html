Every student learns differently and Mathseeds makes it easy to meet each one exactly where they are. 

 As one educator shares: “My students have various gaps in their learning and need repetition of skills. Mathseeds is able to target what they need and give them that practice in a fun and engaging format.” 

 Here's how you can achieve the same results in your classroom: 

🧠 Personalised pathways
Placement tests ensure students start at the perfect level based on ability, not age and can be reset whenever needed. Lessons automatically adjust based on performance, allowing learners to progress at their own pace.  
 
📚 Content for a range of abilities
Over 200 short, structured lessons that build conceptual understanding in number sense, operations, measurement, geometry and more. Varied question types, including visual and auditory prompts, cater to different learning styles and levels. 
 
⚖️ Perfect balance of independence & control 
Students explore activities at their own pace, while teachers maintain full control to assign specific lessons to individuals or groups targeting specific skills. 
 
📊 Data-backed insights that drive action 
Real-time reporting reveals each student's strengths, gaps and growth, helping you group students effectively and provide the support each learner needs. 

 🎯 Built-in motivation & mastery 
Rewards like acorns and pets plus immediate feedback keep students engaged while regular mastery checks ensure retention, automatically redirecting students to review when needed. 

 👩‍🏫 Supports small-group and individual learning 
Perfect for remediation, core instruction or enrichment with scaffolded lessons building foundational skills. Can be used independently at stations while teachers work with small groups.
