Ready to make homework more effective and engaging? Here are proven strategies to help your students succeed with Mathletics at home! 

📝 Set purposeful home activities:
Assign with purpose:
  • Target specific skills and areas for improvement 
  • Set achievable goals for each program 
  • Customise assignments based on individual needs. 
Smart assignment tips: 
  • Keep tasks focused and manageable 
  • Set clear completion deadlines 
  • Include a mix of practice and challenge activities.
🎯 How to assign a Mathletics activity 
  1. Log in to your Teacher Dashboard 
  2. Go to 'Assign and Review' from the left menu 
  3. Select 'Refreshed Assign & Review'
  4. Select 'Learn' 
  5. Pick your 'Class' and 'Group' 
  6. Refine your search with the 'Topic Filter' button on the left 
  7. Select the student and activity box 
  8. Click 'Assign' at the bottom 
  9. Select the start and end date and click ‘Assign’. 
 🏠 Additional homework ideas 
  • Take-home worksheets: Print curriculum-aligned materials from your dashboard
  • Online activities: Assign specific quests or skill-building games 
  • Points competition: Challenge students to reach weekly point goals at home. 
  • Mathletics Parent Pack: This ready-to-send resource helps parents understand the program and includes login details, navigation tips and home learning strategies.
