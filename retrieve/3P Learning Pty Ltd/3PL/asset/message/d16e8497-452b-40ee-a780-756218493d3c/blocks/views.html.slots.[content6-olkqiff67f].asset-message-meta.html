Get more tips for boosting reading fluency

Encourage your students to participate in reading activities both inside and outside the classroom to enhance their fluency and share their reading experiences with others. Here are a few additional strategies to boost reading fluency with your class:

 

  1. Modeled reading practice with a class-wide book:
    • Many books in the Library have a “Read to Me” feature.
  2. Reader’s theater:
    • Assign a book for students to “act out”.
