3 World Math Day preparation tips that work

 

Power up your students World Math Day performance with these handy hints:

 

1. Use Live Mathletics to practice

We can’t stress this enough: Live Mathletics is the competitive engine behind World Math Day, so the more often students compete, the better they get at it.

 

2. Trust in your fingers!

Have your students practice using Live Mathletics without looking at the keyboard – this skill comes in handy for rapid-fire typing!

 

3. Move on if you get it wrong

Students can miss up to 3 answers per Live Mathletics session before it ends. If they get stuck, encourage them to make a guess and move on to the next question – incorrect answers can be reviewed at the end.
