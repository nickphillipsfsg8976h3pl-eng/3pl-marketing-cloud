This week’s Quick Tip [2 mins] 

👉 The best place to start is with a Placement Test to place your students exactly where they need to be in the program.  
  1. Log in to your Teacher Dashboard.  
  2. Click the pink Reading Eggs tile. 
  3. Click ‘Support’ in the top right, search for ‘Assign or reset a placement test’.  
  4. Follow the prompts.
