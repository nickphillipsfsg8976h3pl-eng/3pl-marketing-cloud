In order to clear any confusion, we just wanted to remind all participants that the Mathletics Hall of Fame is different from the March Math Mania leaderboard.

Classes/Country Hall of Fame (please ensure to check under the COUNTRY tab
for student/classes results) is a weekly accumulation of class' points and the clock resets weekly on Saturday around 1pm MT.

Students' daily points are a tally of all points scored in a 24-hour window with the clock on that Hall of Fame resetting daily around 1 pm MT.
