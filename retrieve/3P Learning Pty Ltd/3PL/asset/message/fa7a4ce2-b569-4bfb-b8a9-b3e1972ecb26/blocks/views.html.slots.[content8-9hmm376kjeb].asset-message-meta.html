1. Log in to your Teacher Dashboard.
  2. Click Management on the left-hand side.
  3. Select Manage Students from the list.
  4. Check the box next to the students you want login cards for. 
  5. Hit the Print Logins button.
  6. A PDF document will pop up for you to download. Voilà!
