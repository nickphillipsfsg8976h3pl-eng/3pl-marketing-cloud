This powerful, evidence-based package brings together Reading Eggs, Mathletics and Mathseeds in one seamless platform, making it easier than ever to manage learning, track progress and support every learner – all with one login and school-wide reporting at your fingertips. 
 
3 Essentials means a more rewarding experience for students too! 
 
With single sign-on via QR code or username, students land on a new dashboard with access to all selected programs, teacher-set tasks – and the brand-new motivating Weekly Quest. 

3P Dashboard is currently under development, with rollout to the first group of schools beginning at the start of the new school year.
