Welcome to Term 1!

 

With one in three children failing to meet NAPLAN literacy benchmarks there are calls for a reading revolution across the country. Queensland’s Department of Education is committed to evidence-based instruction, and this is evident in the Department’s Reading Position Statement. It states:

 

Systematic synthetic phonics provides a solid foundation for word reading and writing by teaching the grapheme-phoneme correspondences of SAE in a planned, cumulative sequence. Decodable texts support beginning readers to apply their phonic knowledge and phonemic awareness at text level.

 

We know there’s pressure on teachers, and with more emphasis on the explicit teaching of phonics, many schools and teachers are re-evaluating teaching methods.
