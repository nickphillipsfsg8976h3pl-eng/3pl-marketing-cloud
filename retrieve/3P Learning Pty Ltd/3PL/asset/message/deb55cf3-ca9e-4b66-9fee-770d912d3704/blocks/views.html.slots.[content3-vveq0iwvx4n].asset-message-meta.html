Session Overview:

Getting Started with Narrative Assessment

Date: Wednesday, 28th February

Time: 9am AWST

Location: Online

Duration: 3 hours & 15 minutes

 

This 3-hour online workshop is aimed at new schools/teachers getting started with assessing narrative performances in the Brightpath Ruler.

 

We conclude with a brief section on implementing Brightpath at your school, and where to find Brightpath resources.

 

The 3 hours will be broken in presentation time and time when you will be working on your own or with colleagues.

 

Teachers then have time to assess three scripts before coming back to discuss how they scored the performances; our expert will share how she scored them.

 

Questions and discussion are welcomed through-out the session.
