Every classroom has diverse learning needs. At Seven Oaks School District, Assistant Superintendent Matt Henderson explains how Mathletics helps with equity: "Mathletics gave me an opportunity to really provide extensions for those students who wanted to go deeper and then I was able to spend more time with those students who needed me the most - that's the definition of equity in my book."  

🔧 For struggling students: 
  • Scaffolded learning breaks concepts into manageable steps with guided practice 
  • Interactive feedback and immediate corrections to help students learn from mistakes 
  • Assessment diagnostics pinpoint specific learning gaps with data 
  • 'Courses' customisation tool creates personalised learning paths  
  • 'Fluency' practice targets foundational skills with focused repetition 
  • Small group monitoring tracks progress closely for targeted intervention. 

🚀 For advanced learners: 
  • Enrichment content accesses higher-level materials beyond year expectations
  • 'Problem-Solving & Reasoning' activities develop complex thinking beyond rote skills
  • 'Concept Search' enables self-directed exploration for curious minds 
  • Live Mathletics competitions encourage mastery through competitive practice 
  • Certificate challenges motivate high achievers with advanced achievement goals.
