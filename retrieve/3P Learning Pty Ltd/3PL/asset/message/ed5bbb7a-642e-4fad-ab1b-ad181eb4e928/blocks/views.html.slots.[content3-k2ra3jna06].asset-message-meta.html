üïÆ How to Assign/Reassign the Writing Legends Placement Test:

The Writing Legends Placement test is automatically assigned to students when they first log into Writing Legends. To reset the Placement Test follow these steps:

  1. Sign into your 3 Essentials Dashboard
  2. Senect Writing Legends from the lefthand menu
  3. Click Management
  4. Select Manage Progress from the list
  5. Find the name of the student that you want to reset the placement test for, and select the tickbox on the lefthand side of their name
  6. Click Edit progress and choose Placement Test
  7. Click the Update button

After the placement test is completed, you can review and adjust their levels, if needed through your Wrtiting Legends teacher console.
