1. Log into your Mathletics Teacher Console
  2. Click 'Assign and Review'
  3. Select 'Assess'
  4. Choose the 'Class' and 'Group'
  5. Filter your desired assessment under Curriculum/Activity Filters
  6. Select the Curriculum and Grade/Year
  7. Choose the Assessment
  8. Click 'Assign' at the bottom
