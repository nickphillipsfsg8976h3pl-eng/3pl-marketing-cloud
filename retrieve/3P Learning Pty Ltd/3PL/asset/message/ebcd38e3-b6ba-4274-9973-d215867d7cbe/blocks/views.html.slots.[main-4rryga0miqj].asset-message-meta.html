✍️ Creating writing assignments – quick reminder 

Getting students started on their next writing task is easier than you might think: 
  1. Log in to your 3 Essentials Teacher Dashboard. 
  2. Click the green Writing tile. 
  3. Click Support in the top right and search for Create a writing assignment. 
  4. Follow the prompts as they appear on the screen. 

And that’s it! In less than a minute, your class will have a brand-new writing assignment ready to go.
