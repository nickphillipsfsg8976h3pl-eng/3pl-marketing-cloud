You can easily assign tasks to individual students, groups or classes. Here‚Äôs how to create assignments in Reading Eggs and Reading Eggspress:

  1. Log into your Reading Eggs Teacher Dashboard and toggle to your 3 Essentials page.
  2. Click Reading Eggs
  3. From the left-hand menu, choose the program: Reading Eggs, Reading Eggspress and click Assignments
  4. With all your students visible, filter for an individual year or group and select the student(s) who will receive this assignment
  5. Set the start/finish date
  6. Choose the activity you wish to assign from the dropdown
  7. Review the assignment details
  8. Click +Create this Assignment

Once you have your assignments set up, you can
modify them with just a few clicks:

  1. Sign into Reading Eggsand toggle to the 3 Essentials page
  2. From the left-hand menu, choose the program: Reading Eggs, Reading Eggspress and click Assignments
  3. Click Manage Assignments
  4. Here you can view active or pending assignments (when you've chosen a future start date)
  5. Alternatively, you can expire the assignment, removing it from your students' consoles.
