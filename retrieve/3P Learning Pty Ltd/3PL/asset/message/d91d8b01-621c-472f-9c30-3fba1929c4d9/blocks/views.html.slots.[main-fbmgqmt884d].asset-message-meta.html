Here's how you can do the same! 

📚 Start with ‘Are You Ready’ assessments
Before starting each topic, have your students complete the ‘Are You Ready’ assessment that: 
  • Shows you exactly where each student needs support. 
  • Eliminates guesswork about readiness. 
  • Saves you from re-teaching concepts students already understand. 
Teachers like Katie find this approach helps "give children an independent chance to show what they know and then being able to plug those gaps." 

🎯 Build the weekly habit: 
  • Encourage your students to log in at least 3 times per week. 
  • Challenge them to earn 1000 points through their Weekly Quest.* 
  • Celebrate certificates to maintain momentum!
