In Session 2, Advice from an Expert in How to Use Writing Assessment to Drive Learning, the discussion centred around the effective utilisation of Brightpath's formative assessments, and its role in empowering students to take charge of their own learning. Carmel Spruhan, a teacher at Larrakeyah Primary School and Chrissy Marcelli, a teacher at Southern Grove Primary School, joined us and you can watch their presentation highlights to learn more.
