Have students low on confidence?

 

Many students struggle with confidence because they feel like they are not getting the content and never will. Comparing ourselves to each other is also ingrained in us and our students aren't immune to this. Too often, they compare their own progress to their peers and can label themselves as 'dumb' if they are not progressing as fast.

 

The beauty of EdTech tools is that students learn at their own pace on their personalised pathway and are rewarded for their effort. Activities are always in their zone of proximal development so they don't get too frustrated OR bored. Plus, they adore being able to see their progress and aceing their own goals.
