The 2025 school year is almost here! Let's kick things off by completing the rollover and ensuring that all your students are in the right classes for the year.

 

What is Rollover?

 

The Rollover process enables you to make necessary changes to last year's school roll, including adding or removing students and updating class information. Completing the rollover ensures your teachers and students are assigned to the correct classes and grade levels as they start using Mathletics for the new academic year.

 

Note: If you have already completed your Mathletics Rollover for this year or are not at the start of your school year, please disregard this email.
