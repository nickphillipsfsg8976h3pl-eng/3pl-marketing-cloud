Mathletics gives you full access to a collection of teaching resources to help you save time lesson planning, challenge and engage your students and track progress all in one place! Our downloadable workbooks and activity sheets cover a wide range of core maths concepts and are perfect for additional classroom work or as homework for your students.

 

To access these resources, log in and click on the 'Resource Hub' link in the left navigation bar on your Teacher Console. From there, you can enter the Mathletics Library and filter the resources by year level.
