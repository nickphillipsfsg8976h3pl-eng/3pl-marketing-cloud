Recently Mathseeds partnered with LearnPlatform to analyze the efficacy of our program and found that K–2 students who had high usage in Mathseeds had higher end-of-year NWEA MAP® achievement compared to students who had low use of the program. Read more about this study and our ESSA accreditation.
