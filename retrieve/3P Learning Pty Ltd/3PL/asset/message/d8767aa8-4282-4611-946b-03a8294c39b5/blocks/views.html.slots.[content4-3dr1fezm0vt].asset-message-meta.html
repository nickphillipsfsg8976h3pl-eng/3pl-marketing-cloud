2. Golden Eggs and Avatars

 

Students have their own avatar, a little character that represents them, and can earn golden eggs for completing activities. They can spend them in the Plaza or Mall to purchase outfits to dress up their avatar and accessories and furniture to redecorate their house or apartment.

 

We offer introductory sessions but also deeper dives that explore a particular feature or goal. We also answer all your questions!
