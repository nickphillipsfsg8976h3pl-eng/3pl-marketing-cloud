3. The Playroom and Stadium

 

In Reading Eggs, students can also use the Golden Eggs they've earned to play games in the Play Room. This is a great way to finish off a lesson on a fun note or reward them for completing work.

 

Reading Eggspress students can enter the Stadium to compete in literacy games against their classmates or even their peers around the world!
