Thrive & Grow Plans: Own Your Development

There's a reason Creating Lifelong Learners is a 3P value and a pillar of what we do. Continuous learning and development is good for us as people – and honestly, it’s great for 3P too. 

In our 2024 Pea-ple Voice survey we had our highest overall engagement result to date (80%), driven by high favorability scores between 86-94% related to work life blend, 3P values, individual managers, inclusion and social connection. Particularly, relating to development, feedback and recognition our scores were 73% favourable. So, in December 2024, we launched the Thrive & Grow Portal on 3PYOU - a personalised development hub designed to guide and empower you in shaping your own growth journey.
