What is a Thrive & Grow Plan? 

The Thrive & Grow Plan is a flexible, voluntary tool that helps you reflect on where you've been, where you are, and where you want to go. With simple prompts, the tool supports you in identifying key development areas - whether that’s optimising current contributions (Thrive Now) or preparing for new challenges ahead (Future-Ready).

Why use the Thrive & Grow Plan tool? 

  • 🤔 Reflect: Use it to assess your growth, achievements, and focus areas.

  • 📝 Plan: Identify specific development goals and create a roadmap for action based on the 3 Es of Lifelong Learning - Experience, Exposure, and Education.

  • 💬 Discuss: It provides a great foundation for meaningful development conversations with your manager.

  • 🎯 Develop: Set goals, track progress, and take ownership of your growth with a clear actionable plan.

How Do You Get Started? 

The tool and supporting resources are available on the Thrive & Grow Portal in 3PYOU. It's entirely voluntary, so why not take the initiative to have a go at creating your own Thrive & Grow Plan?
