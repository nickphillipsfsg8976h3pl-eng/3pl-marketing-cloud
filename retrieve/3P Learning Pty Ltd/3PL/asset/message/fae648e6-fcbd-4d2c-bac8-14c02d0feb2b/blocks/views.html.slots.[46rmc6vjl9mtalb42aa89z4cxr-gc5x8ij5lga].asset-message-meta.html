Pea-flex in Action: Greetings from Broome!

At its core, Pea-flex empowers our people with choice – where we work, how we work, and the freedom to balance work and life in a way that works best for us, our roles and our teams.

Flexibility at 3P is built on trust, balance, and personal ownership, allowing our team to thrive in ways that suit our lives whilst also delivering great work for the business. 

This edition, we’re spotlighting Alastair Pharo, one of our Senior Software Engineers, who has embraced the concept of flexibility in his recent relocation to Broome, Western Australia. 

As always, if you're considering moving to a different state of country, whether temporarily or permanently, be sure to discuss it with your manager in advance for approval - understanding that due to practical, legal, insurance, or accounting reasons, 3P may not be able to support all requests.
