Every student learns differently and Mathletics makes it easy to meet each one exactly where they are. 

At Calvary Christian School, teacher Elise Vargas discovered exactly this: "Mathletics does a really great job of targeting students where they're at and where they need to go next. It allows us to differentiate and extend those students who need to go further by using multiple aspects of the program." 

Here's how you can achieve the same results in your classroom: 

✅ Create ability groups to assign differentiated content based on skill levels. 
✅ Customise practice for each student by targeting areas for improvement. 
✅ Set individual activities using 'Assign & Review' including revision tasks & Skill Quests. 
✅ Address knowledge gaps immediately and track progress with data-backed reports.

🔧 Fill Learning Gaps tool: This smart tool analyses Assessment results and recommends Understanding, Practice & Fluency activities (Skill Quests) to address areas where students need support. This feature is available in the Assessments area, under the Class and Group dropdown.  

👥 How to create tailored learning groups: 
  1. Log in to your Mathletics Teacher Dashboard 
  2. Click 'Manage Students' 
  3. Select 'Groups', then 'New Group' 
  4. Fill in the new group information 
  5. Add students to their ability group by using the dropdown and selecting the appropriate group. 
Many schools find that combining Mathletics differentiation with Reading Eggs creates comprehensive support for students who need foundational skills across both numeracy and literacy. You can trial Reading Eggs right from your 3P Dashboard. Log in to learn more.
