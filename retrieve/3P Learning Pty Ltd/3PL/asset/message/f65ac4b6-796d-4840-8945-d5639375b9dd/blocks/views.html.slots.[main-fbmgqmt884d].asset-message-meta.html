Ready to make homework more effective and engaging? Here are proven strategies to help your students succeed with Reading Eggs at home! 

📝 Set purposeful home activities: 
Assign with purpose: 
  • Target specific skills and areas for improvement 
  • Set achievable goals for each program 
  • Customise assignments based on individual needs.  
Smart assignment tips: 
  • Keep tasks focused and manageable 
  • Set clear completion deadlines 
  • Include a mix of practice and challenge activities. 
One teacher shares the parent feedback they've received: "We’ve had parents saying, 'Wow, this is actually great… they're really learning!' They truly appreciate the program's positive impact, with students now eager to get home and practice on Reading Eggs." 

🎯 How to assign a lesson for your students to complete at home 
  1. Log in to your Teacher Dashboard 
  2. Click ‘Assignments’ then ‘Create Assignment’ 
  3. Select your class or specific students 
  4. Add a start and finish date 
  5. Select your year/grade level and then Reading, Spelling or Storylands Lesson 
  6. Click ‘Create this assignment’. 
🏠 Additional homework ideas 
  • Take-home worksheets: Print curriculum-aligned materials from your dashboard 
  • Online activities: Assign driving tests, games and puzzles for extra practice 
  • Points competition: Challenge students to reach weekly point goals at home 
  • Reading Eggs Parent Letter: This ready-to-send resource helps parents understand the program and includes login details, navigation tips and home learning strategies.
