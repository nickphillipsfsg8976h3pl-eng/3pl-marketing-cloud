✍️ Writing Legends is our online writing program for students in years 1–6.
It’s created by education experts to boost student writing skills and simplify writing instruction for teachers through:

  • carefully sequenced, engaging lessons
  • interactive, scaffolded learning and manageable learning activities
  • hundreds of exciting writing prompts to spark creativity
  • instant feedback to help students improve.

Together, they make literacy learning seamless, fun and effective. Plus, with data-driven progress tracking, you can target teaching to meet the needs of every student in your class.
