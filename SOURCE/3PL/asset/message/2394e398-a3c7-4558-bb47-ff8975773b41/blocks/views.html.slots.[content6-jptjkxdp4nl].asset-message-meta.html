Other teacher resources

 

The yearly planners suggest a program of learning using Mathseeds and Mathseeds Prime for years K-4. They list everything Mathseeds and Mathseeds Prime offer to supplement your teaching program, from the online lessons to our Mental Minute fluency program and Driving Tests assessment program, as well as printable resources on every topic.

 

For every Lessons, we have worksheets and problem-solving activities. In our Resources sections, we have more worksheets, posters to put up in the classroom, and other useful maths resources to print and make. We have a whole section of Printable Assessments as well as a Big Books library to help you introduce each topic to your class.
 

Our Printables Library is also full of great resources – flashcards, learning games, fluency workbooks to go with Mental Minute, plus workbooks and teacher books for all strands of mathematics and at each year level from K-4.
 

Mathseeds is here to help your maths planning go as smoothly as possible to maintain the high level of teaching and learning we know you want to provide for your students.

 

P.S. Need help implementing? You can find our Mathseeds Best Practice Guide online or reach out to your Account Manager for more information.
