Why should you use this pack?

 

Many teachers fall into the trap of ending a learning topic with a formative assessment, recording the results and analysing what needs to be reviewed etc. But for the student, it's as if everything comes to a screeching halt and many don't give the topic a second thought.

 

However, this is actually a crucial period and doing a little self-reflecting can have a big impact. Seize the opportunity to help your students deepen their learning, refocus their goals and renew their confidence.

 

Tracking progress and self-reflection go hand in hand with EdTech.
 

Many EdTech programs handle tracking and reporting on lessons, activities and assessments. But did you know that some also place students on personalised learning pathways and provide instant feedback to encourage them to see their own progress? Find out more here.
