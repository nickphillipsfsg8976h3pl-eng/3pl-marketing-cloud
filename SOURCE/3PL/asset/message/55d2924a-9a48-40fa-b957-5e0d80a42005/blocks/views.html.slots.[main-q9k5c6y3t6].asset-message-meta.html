When Jane shared the news of her retirement, there were tears, mine especially. Tears of joy for her to focus on her health and her charitable passions, and tears of fear for how much we would miss her wisdom and steady presence. Jane has been the reassuring face during tough product delays, and the same smiling face when exciting updates were delivered. 

Her passion for giving back also helped spark our company-wide Giving Back initiative. She drove conversations that set it in motion, while continuing her own projects focused on education and empowering those with less access to learning. 

Jane Fisher, you have been an absolute force for good in your nearly 10 years at 3P Learning. We cannot thank you enough, and we hope this next chapter brings you the time, health, and joy you so deeply deserve.
