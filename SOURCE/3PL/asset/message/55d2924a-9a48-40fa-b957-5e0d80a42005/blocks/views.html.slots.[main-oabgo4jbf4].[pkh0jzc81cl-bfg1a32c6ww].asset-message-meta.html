Celebrating 15 Years with
Laura Anderson

This month, the editorial team were in floods of tears as we celebrated Laura Anderson’s retirement. To Blake and then 3PL, Laura brought her exceptional talent as a writer and teacher to all areas of Reading Eggspress and Writing Legends. To our team, Laura was that and so much more. She was our in-house grammarian, linguist, War of Roses specialist, book reviewer, intrepid traveller, sailor, spiritual guide, friend, confidant and shining light.
