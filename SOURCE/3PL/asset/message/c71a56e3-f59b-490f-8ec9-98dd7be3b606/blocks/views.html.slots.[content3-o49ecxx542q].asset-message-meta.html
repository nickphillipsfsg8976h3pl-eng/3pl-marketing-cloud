Effortless access made simple:

 

Ensuring smooth access for your class is a breeze. Your class can login using their Reading Eggs or Mathseeds usernames and passwords OR:

 
  1. Log into your Writing Legends Teacher Dashboard
  2. In the Navigation panel, click on 'Management' then Manage Students'
  3. Select the students needing login cards
  4. Hit 'Print Logins' for ready-to-use login cards!
