We're excited to welcome you back for another incredible school year! It's time to renew your Mathletics subscription for the 2024-25 school year. Mathletics remains a fully aligned curriculum resource, packed with tools to enhance your classroom experience.

To complete your renewal, please fill out the form HERE.



Here’s what you can expect from Mathletics this year:

  • ON curriculum content from K-10, with student access both at home and school, including new content for grades 1-9

  • Assessments (Grades 2-8) with the 'Fill Learning Gaps' feature. Watch our Fill Learning Gaps Video.

  • Built-in student support for curriculum-based activities

  • Easy Classroom Management to accommodate diverse learning environments and pathways

  • Partnership with Marian Small – Rich Learning Tasks for K-8

  • Diagnostic and formative assessment tools

  • E-books and digital resources K-12 – printable

  • 700+ Problem Solving & Reasoning activities in a modern learning environment that encourages students to think critically and collaborate. Watch our PSR Video.

  • Numeracy for Early Learners (K-3) – focusing on numeracy and number sense with videos, games, and storybooks. Watch our Numeracy for Early Learners Video.

  • Ongoing teacher support

  • The Mathletics Parent Support Pack.

  • The Mathletics Knowledge Base for on-demand support, classroom printables, and more!

  • Compliance with privacy legislation – our servers are based in Montreal. For more information, visit our Privacy Policy.



We look forward to another fantastic year with you and your students!


Best regards,
The Mathletics Team
