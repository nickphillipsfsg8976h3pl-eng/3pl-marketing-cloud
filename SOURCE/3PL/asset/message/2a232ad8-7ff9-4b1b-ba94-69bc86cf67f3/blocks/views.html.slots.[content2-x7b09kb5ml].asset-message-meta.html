ðŸ’­What is Fast Phonics?

  

%%=v(@NAME)=%%

Fast Phonics is a complete systematic, synthetic phonics programme that incorporates best practice research to engage and motivate pupils who are learning to read and spell. In a Systematic Synthetic Phonics program, students will focus on learning the grapheme/phoneme combinations, blending sounds to make words and segmenting them to spell.

You and your students have access to the Fast Phonics program within the Reading Eggs console. Fast Phonics includes access to an engaging online programme with accompanying resources for teachers, including lesson plans, support videos, slides, printable phonics and handwriting sheets as well as printable phonics reading books.
