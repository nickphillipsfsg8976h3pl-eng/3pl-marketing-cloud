Digital Big Books are books you can put up on the big screen for front-of-class demonstrations. It's a great way to keep students engaged.
 

1. Click Teacher Toolkit.
 

2. Click Big Books.

 

3. Select your year.
 

4. Choose your book.
 

5. Read out loud, ask questions or turn on the audio at the top of the book.
