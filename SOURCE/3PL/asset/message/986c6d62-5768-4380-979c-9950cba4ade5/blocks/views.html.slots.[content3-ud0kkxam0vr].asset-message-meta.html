Editing student passwords

 

You may wish to also edit your student passwords to make logging in faster.

 

1. Select the students you wish to edit.
 

2. Click the eye icon next to password.
 

3. Click the pencil icon and type the new password in.
 

4. Click the green tick to save the new password.
