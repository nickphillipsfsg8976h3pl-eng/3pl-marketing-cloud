What is Reading Eggs?

Built on robust research and proven instructional strategies, Reading Eggs is our award-winning online reading program. Reading Eggs harnesses the five essential pillars of the Science of Reading: phonics, phonemic awareness, vocabulary, fluency and comprehension.
