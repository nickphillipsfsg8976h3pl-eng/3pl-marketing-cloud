How to begin your students' journey


Using the login cards, have your students sign-in to the program. Mathseeds uses a progressive lesson sequence tailored to each student’s ability level. When a student first starts their Mathseeds journey, they’ll be asked to complete a Placement Test to assesses what they already know and ensure they begin at the appropriate level. Teachers are able to reset the Placement Test or move students to a different level in the ‘Manage Students’ section.

If you have any questions, our Getting Started Guide covers everything in more detail. You can also reach out to one of our Education Specialists above and they’ll be happy to help you out!
