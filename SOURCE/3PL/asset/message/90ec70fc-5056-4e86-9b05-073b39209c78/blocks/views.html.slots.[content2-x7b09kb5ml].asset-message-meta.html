We just wanted to let you know that our rollover period is coming to a close soon.

While you can still complete rollover at any time, ticking it off now means that you can make us do all the work! We've got a team on hand to sort out all the admin and ensure that your students are set up in the correct classes.

So, if you'd like to save some time and make us do the pesky admin, follow the steps below!

Rollover is even easier this year! Simply download this empty spreadsheet, fill it in, and send it back to us at rollover@3plearning.com and we will take care of the rest.

How to complete your Wordflyers Rollover



  1. Download this empty spreadsheet

  2. Fill it in with your updated class details

  3. Send it back to us at rollover@3plearning.com, and we will take care of the rest!

For data security purposes, we ask that you encrypt the file with a password in a separate email.

  1. Select 'File' → 'Info'

  2. Select the 'Protect Workbook' box and choose 'Encrypt with Password'

  3. Enter a password in the Password box, and select 'OK'

  4. Confirm the password in the 'Re-enter Password' box, and then select 'OK'.

DOWNLOAD YOUR WORDFLYERS SPREADSHEET


If you are not your school's WordFlyers Administrator, please click here to let us know. This will help ensure we have the correct Administrator and Subscription Coordinator for your school.

The rollover team is processing rollovers with a three-day turnaround. If you have any questions or need any assistance, please reach out to our dedicated Rollover Support Team at rollover@3plearning.com or customer.support@3plearning.com. We're here to help you every step of the way.

For immediate support:

Please visit the WordFlyers Rollover document or watch this video to learn more.

Thank you for choosing WordFlyers as your education partner.
