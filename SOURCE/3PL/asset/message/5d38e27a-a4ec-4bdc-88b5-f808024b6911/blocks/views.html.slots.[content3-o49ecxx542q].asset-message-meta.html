Giving access to your students is simple:

 

Your class can log in using their ABC Reading Eggs or ABC Mathseeds usernames and passwords.

 

Alternatively, you can print new login cards by following these steps:
 
  1. Log in to your ABC Reading Eggs and ABC Mathseeds account.
  2. Click the blue Writing tab in the top left of your Teacher Dashboard.
  3. In the Navigation panel, click on 'Management' then Manage Students'.
  4. Select the students needing login cards.
  5. Hit 'Print Logins' for ready-to-use login cards!
