Step 1 Updating Teachers


Remove Teachers who are no longer at your school

  • Select teachers you would like to delete
  • Click ‘More actions’
  • Click ‘Delete teacher accounts’
Add new teachers
  • Click ‘Create single teacher’
  • Fill out information and click ‘Create’

New teachers will be sent a welcome email with instructions on creating a password to access their new account.

Send existing teachers a password reset link

  • Select existing teachers
  • Click ‘More actions’
  • Select ‘Email password reset’

*Usernames no longer work. Teachers must use their email address to login.

Step 2 Updating students


Remove Students who are no longer at your school

  • Select students you would like to delete
  • Click ‘More actions’
  • Click ‘Delete student accounts’

Adding new students to your school

For adding a single student, select ‘Create single student, fill out information and click ‘Create’. For adding multiple students,

  • Click ‘Create multiple students’
  • Click ‘Download template’ and fill out
  • Copy and paste the filled-out information from the template into the table

Note: Please ensure column order matches the template, and ‘External ID’ is not mandatory but can be used if your school has external ID’s.

Increment student year levels

  • Click the ‘All students dropdown’ select ‘unassigned’
  • Select ‘All’ students and then ‘’More actions’
  • Select ‘Increment year level’

*If you have students you wish to remain in the same grade/year level, simply click edit next to the students to update.


Step 3 Updating classes

Delete old classes. *If for any reason you have a class where students will continue their work in, do NOT delete that class.

  • Click ‘Classes’
  • Select ‘All’
  • Click ‘Delete’
Create new classes and add students.
  • Click ‘Classes’
  • Select teacher for the class
  • Select one or more subscriptions(products) for the class
  • Enter class details
  • Move students across to the new class
  • Repeat steps for each class

Step 4 Download student login cards

  • Click ‘Students’
  • Select ‘All’
  • Click ‘Print logins’
