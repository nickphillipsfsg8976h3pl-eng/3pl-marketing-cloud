How to check results


 
  • From your Teacher Console, click ‘Reports’
  • In the drop down, you’ll have many different reports to choose from. To see how your students performed on a Driving Test, click ‘Driving Tests’
  • From here, you can see how all of your students performed or you can look at results from individual students
