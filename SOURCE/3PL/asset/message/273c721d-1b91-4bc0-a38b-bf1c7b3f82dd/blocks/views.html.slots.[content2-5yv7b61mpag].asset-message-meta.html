See the key features for easy differentiation in Mathletics:

  • Personalised instruction: Mathletics enables teachers to differentiate instruction for individual students or small groups based on their abilities. All to maximise student engagement and motivate them by empowerment.
  • Varied resources: With unlimited access to the Printables library, teachers never have to worry about sourcing printable and digital teaching aids adequate to their students’ learning needs.
  • Customisable learning paths: In Mathletics, teachers can set minimum levels according to students’ abilities and manually adjust them as students progress.
