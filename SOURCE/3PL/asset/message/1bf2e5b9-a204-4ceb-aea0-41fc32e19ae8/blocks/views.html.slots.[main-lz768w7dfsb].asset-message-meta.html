🪄 Create targeted assignments with just a few clicks  
 Save time and set assignments directly from the program!  

You can assign specific Lessons to guide your students toward content that aligns with what they’re learning in class.  Whether for homework or in-class activities, you gain greater control and confidence in directing your students’ focus.   
  1. Log in to your 3 Essentials Teacher Dashboard.  
  2. Click the green Writing tile.   
  3. Click Support in the top right and search for Create a writing assignment. 
  4. Follow the prompts as they appear on the screen.
