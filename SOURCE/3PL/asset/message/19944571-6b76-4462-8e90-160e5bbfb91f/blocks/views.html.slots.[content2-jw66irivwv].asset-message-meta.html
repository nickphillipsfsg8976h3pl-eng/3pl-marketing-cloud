3 ways Mathletics can make personalised learning simplier
 
  1. Mathletics gives you better visibility of all the little things that make up individual student progress. Along with assessment and acitivity results, Mathletics can show you how many times a student attempted an activity, participation and engagement levels, homework completed and more.
  2. Creating personalised learning pathways has never been simplier because personalisation is inbuilt into Mathletics. For example, each student is always kept in their Zone of Proximal Development, meaning that they only ever shown content that is just right for them - not too hard and not too easy.
  3. Students co-create their own learning journeys. Along with curriculum-aligned activities and assessments you set, students have the option to play Live Mathletics, Multiverse or Rainforest Maths. Students love these games and are often highly engaged in spending extra time practicing their skills and furthering their own learning journey.
