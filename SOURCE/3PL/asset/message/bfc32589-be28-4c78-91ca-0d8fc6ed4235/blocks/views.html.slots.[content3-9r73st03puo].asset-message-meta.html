The engagement factor
 

It's a no-brainer that students will be more like to retain knowledge and skills if they are engaged while learning it. That's why Mathletics was designed to engage students right off the bat. Live Mathletics gets students pumped to practice while Meritopia is the perfect blend of intrinsic and extrinstic rewards to keep them going.
