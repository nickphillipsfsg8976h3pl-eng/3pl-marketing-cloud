In preparation for World Maths Day, please log in to your account today to ensure you can set up your classes (if needed) and that your students can access Live Mathletics within the Mathletics program.

 

▪️If your school account is using the 3 Essentials dashboard (you will automatically see this when you log in), you can participate under your school account.

  • If your school has a Mathletics subscription, reach out to your administrator or subscription coordinator if needed to confirm your access. Follow these steps to set up any new classes participating in World Maths Day. Existing Mathletics classes are already set up to participate.
  • If your school doesn’t have a Mathletics subscription, you will be able to participate in World Maths Day through a free trial, which has been set up for you. Follow these steps to set up any new classes participating in World Maths Day.

▪️If multiple teachers and classes are participating, we recommend using one school account.
