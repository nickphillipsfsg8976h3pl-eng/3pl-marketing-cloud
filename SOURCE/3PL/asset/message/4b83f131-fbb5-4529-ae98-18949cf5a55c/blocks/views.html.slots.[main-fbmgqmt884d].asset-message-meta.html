While Reading Eggs is excellent for independent learning, it's equally powerful for front-of-class instruction. 

From interactive whiteboard lessons to whole-class phonics demonstrations, these tools transform traditional teaching into engaging, interactive experiences that reach every learning style. 

One educator explains how Reading Eggs supports their teaching: "It gives me resources and lessons for whole class instruction as well as independent learning and practice. I find it invaluable!" 

Ready to integrate Reading Eggs into your classroom routine? 
📚 In-class strategies for early readers: Reading Eggs and Fast Phonics
  • Perfect for: K–2 readers during literacy centres and guided reading. 
  • Classroom integration:  
    • Use for 15-minute independent rotations while you work with small groups. 
    • Perfect for differentiated stations targeting the five pillars of reading. 
    • Great for whole-class phonics warm-ups and demonstrations. 
  • Best classroom practice:  
    • Set up as a literacy centre during guided reading time. 
    • Use for fast finishers or early arrival activities. 
    • Display student progress on classroom charts for motivation. 
📖 In-class strategies for advanced readers: Reading Eggspress
  •  Perfect for: Grades 2–6 during independent reading time. 
  • Classroom integration:  
    • Ideal for reading centres and choice time. 
    • Use comprehension quizzes for quick formative assessments. 
    • Assign during silent reading periods. 
  • Best classroom practice:  
    • Integrate into daily 5 rotations. 
    • Use for extension activities when students finish early. 
    • Create classroom competitions around comprehension achievements.
