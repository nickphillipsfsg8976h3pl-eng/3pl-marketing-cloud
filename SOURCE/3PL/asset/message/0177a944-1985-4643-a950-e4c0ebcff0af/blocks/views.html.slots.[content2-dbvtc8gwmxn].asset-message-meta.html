The Parent Hub

First things first, the Parent Hub! This is where you can manage your subscription and activate your trial.
 

Tip: Your username is your email address. You should have received a separate email with instructions on how to reset your password.
 
 

Activating your trial

 

To activate your trial, you'll need to assign a subscription to your child. Don't worry, I know that sounds complicated but there will be instructions helping you along the way! Once you're logged in, under the manage subscription section, simply click on the yellow “Add Student” button.
 
 

Now here comes the fun bit - getting your child started on their Mathletics journey!
