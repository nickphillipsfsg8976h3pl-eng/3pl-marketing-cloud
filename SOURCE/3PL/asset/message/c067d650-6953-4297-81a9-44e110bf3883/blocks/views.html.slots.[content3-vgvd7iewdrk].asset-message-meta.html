Join us as we welcome Alison Ryan M.Ed. Alison is a literacy specialist, teacher educator, and founder of Learning At The Primary Pond Inc. Her focus is to help educators bridge the gap between literacy research and practical classroom strategies.

In this session we dive deep into the principles and characteristics that administrators need to know for effective phonics instruction in K-3 classrooms.
