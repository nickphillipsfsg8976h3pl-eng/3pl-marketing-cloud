In case you missed the game-changing Mathletics update: early in 2024 we rolled out New Courses – an entirely new learning journey, grounded in High-Impact Teaching Strategies (HITS) and Explicit Teaching.

 

New content for Years 3–6 is developed specifically for the updated Australian Curriculum and its state counterparts, with an aim to deepen student understanding through:

 

✨ Utilising HITS and Explicit Teaching

 

✨ At the Unit level, New Courses enables students to systematically build their knowledge and skills with a well-structured learning pathway, each question set building knowledge and skills in a clear progression

 

✨ Well-structured question sets: Within each 10-question set, the structure is always the same:

  • 5 fluency questions encourage students to recall new concepts and skills, and calculate efficiently
  • 3 reasoning questions focus on analysing, proving, evaluating and explaining
  • 2 problem-solving questions, where students develop the ability to interpret, formulate, model and investigate problem situations

✨ Each question set is accompanied by a Concept Refresh Video, and every question has a hint and a worked solution, which all provides a structured, supportive and predictable learning experience

 

✨ All infused with Mathletics’ trademark: FUN!
