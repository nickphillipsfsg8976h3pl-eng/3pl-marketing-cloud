• Placement test is where every student starts their learning journey within Mathseeds. It ensures every child is working at the right level according to their needs and skills.
  • Customizable learning paths: Teachers can change student levels at any time, i.e., after assessment.
  • Personalized instruction: Self-paced, step-by-step lessons provide students with individualised lessons at their level.
