🎉Cross Program Reporting is Here!

Get the information you need, much faster and easier! Cross Program Reporting is now available in 3 Essentials, giving teachers and school leaders a quick yet comprehensive overview of student activity across all programs in a single report. 
 
This means you can: 
 
Save time: Get top-level usage data across all programs from one file. 

Get better insights: Quickly identify usage patterns across your school and programs. 

Easily filter the info you’re after: Customise reports by program, class, year group and date ranges.
