Why do we need student self-reflection?
 

Amidst the pressure to catch up on the curriculum, we often fall back on summative assessments to pinpoint learning gaps. Yet, smaller, formative assessments can be just as insightful. This helps you see how students view their own progression, and pushes them to think analytically about their own learning experiences, identify strengths and weaknesses and refocus their goals.
