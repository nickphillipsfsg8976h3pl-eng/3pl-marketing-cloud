Please complete the Rollover Process



%%=v(@NAME)=%%

We want to help you get off to a flying start to the new school year. So this year, you can simply download and fill in this empty spreadsheet with your new class details and send it in to rollover@3plearning.com. We'll take care of making sure your students are all in the right classes. Easy peasy!

Rollover is even easier this year! Simply download this empty spreadsheet, fill it in, and send it back to us at rollover@3plearning.com and we will take care of the rest.

If your school uses both Mathseeds and Reading Eggs, please complete and send us your Reading Eggs Rollover Spreadsheet first, followed by your Mathseeds Rollover Spreadsheet.

How to complete your Mathseeds Rollover



  1. Download this empty spreadsheet

  2. Fill it in with your updated class details

  3. Send it back to us at rollover@3plearning.com, and we will take care of the rest!

For data security purposes, we ask that you encrypt the file with a password in a separate email.

  1. Select 'File' → 'Info'

  2. Select the 'Protect Workbook' box and choose 'Encrypt with Password'

  3. Enter a password in the Password box, and select 'OK'

  4. Confirm the password in the 'Re-enter Password' box, and then select 'OK'.

DOWNLOAD YOUR MATHSEEDS SPREADSHEET


If you have any questions or need any assistance, please reach out to our dedicated Rollover Support Team at rollover@3plearning.com. We're here to help you every step of the way.

For immediate support:

Please visit the Mathseeds Knowledge Base and search for 'Rollover'.

If you've recieved this email but are not the appropriate contact, click here to let us know.

Thank you for your attention to this crucial task to ensure a seamless start for all your students and teachers.

Best regards,
The Mathseeds Support Team
