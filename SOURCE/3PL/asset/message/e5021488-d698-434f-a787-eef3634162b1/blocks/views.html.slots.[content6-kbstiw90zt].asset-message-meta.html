To access Writing Legends, set up your students through your Reading Eggs or Mathseeds account. Here's how:
 
  1. Log in to your Reading Eggs or Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on the ‘Writing’ tab located at the top left of the dashboard
  3. In the Navigation panel, click on 'Management' then 'Manage Students'
  4. Select 'Copy Classes' then click 'Copy'
  5. You and your students can enjoy free access
