Session 2: Advice from an Expert in How to Use Writing Assessment to Drive Learning

Date: Monday, 11th September

Time: 4pm AEST
 

Building on the insights from Session 1, this session delves into practical guidance for translating writing assessments into enhanced student learning experiences.

Assessment serves as the bridge between teaching and learning. Discover how to leverage writing assessments to drive impactful learning outcomes in your school. Our experts will share invaluable strategies and resources that empower educators to seamlessly integrate assessment insights into their teaching practices.

 

Presenter: Dr Sandy Heldsinger, Brightpath Progress Founder & Assessment Expert

Dr. Sandy Heldsinger, a trailblazer in education assessment, has led WA's system-level assessments and taught master's level courses in the field. Her resource development, including innovative reporting software Brightpath Progress, empowers schools to optimise assessment for student success. Sandy's influence extends to curriculum shaping, notably co-authoring "What Teachers Need to Know About Assessment and Reporting." Her dedication earned her the WAPPA Service Award in 2016 for her significant impact on education.
 

Co-Presenters: Carmel Spruhan, Year 5/6 Teacher at Larrakeyah Primary School & Rachel Pendal, Year 4 Teacher, Curriculum & Assessment Co-ordinator atKalamunda Christian School.
