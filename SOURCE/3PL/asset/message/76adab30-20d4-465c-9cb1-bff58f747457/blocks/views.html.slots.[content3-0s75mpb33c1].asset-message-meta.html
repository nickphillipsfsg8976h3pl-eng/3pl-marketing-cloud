Session 2: Advice from an expert in how to use writing assessment to drive learning

Date: Monday, 11th September

Time: 4pm AEST
 

Guest Speakers: Carmel Spruhan - Year 5/6 Teacher at Larrakeyah Primary School. She worked with the Brightpath team to introduce assessment of writing practices to Larrakeyah Primary School and is a founding member of the Teacher Practice Reference Group for the Online Formative Assessment initiative.

 

Rachel Pendal - Year 4 Teacher, Curriculum & Assessment Co-ordinator at Kalamunda Christian School. She has accumulated 30 years of experience working in independent schools and possesses a strong passion for Literacy.
