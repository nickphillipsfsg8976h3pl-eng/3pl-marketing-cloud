Early in 2024, we rolled out New Courses – an entirely new learning journey, grounded in High-Impact Teaching Strategies and Explicit Teaching.

 

Offering a perfect blend of challenge, engagement and research-based pedagogy, New Courses helps students develop fluency, problem-solving and reasoning skills with a proven 10-question set structure – all while keeping the fun factor intact!

 

New Courses:
 

⚡uses chunking and learning sequences to build skills

⚡is fully aligned with the Australian Curriculum, Version 9.0 and your state’s curriculum or syllabus – wherever you teach in Australia

⚡follows a scope and sequence that support the sequential acquisition of key maths skills, concepts and knowledge

⚡presents carefully scaffolded questions in a logical learning progression, and fully explains and demonstrates what students need to learn

⚡offers high-quality and effective feedback

⚡includes learning videos, hints and worked solutions.
