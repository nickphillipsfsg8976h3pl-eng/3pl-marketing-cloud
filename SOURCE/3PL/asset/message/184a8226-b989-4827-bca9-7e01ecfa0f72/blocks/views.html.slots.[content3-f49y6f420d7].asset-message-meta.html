Front-of-class activities
 

Using Student View to demonstrate activities, complete them together as a class or watch the videos are all great ways to introduce or review a topic.

 

Simply display the program on the big screen and go to the Explore tab of the Student Console. From here you'll be about to browse and select Quests, activities or videos.
