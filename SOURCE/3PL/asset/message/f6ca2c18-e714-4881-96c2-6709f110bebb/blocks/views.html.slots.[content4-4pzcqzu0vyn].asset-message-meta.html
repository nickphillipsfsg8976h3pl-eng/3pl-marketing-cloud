To access your reports just follow these steps:

  1. Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click 'Reports’
  3. Select 'Dashboard'
  4. Toggle to 'Table' at the top of the screen to drill down to student results
  5. Click on the student’s name
  6. Click 'Export' to print a full report
