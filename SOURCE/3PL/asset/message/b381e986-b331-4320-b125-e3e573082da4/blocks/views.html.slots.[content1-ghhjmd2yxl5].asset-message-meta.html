The Mathletics data reset will occur mid-July for this school year. What does this mean?

  • Results of Activities (Activity Mastery activities), Skill Quests (formerly Understanding, Practice & Fluency tasks), and Challenges (Problem-Solving & Reasoning tasks) in the ‘To do’ area, and progress bars are all reset in student accounts.
  • Any assigned tasks or activities are reset.
  • Any certificates, points and M Coins in the student console will also reset at this time
  • Certificate achievement levels will also be reset so that students start from Bronze. Certificates awarded during the 2025-2026 school year will still be accessible in the Teacher and Student Centres.
