In preparation for the 2025-2026 school year, we have put together some tips and tricks to ensure a smooth start when you and your students are back from the summer holidays:

  • Use your email for your login credentials. To create a better experience for all of our teachers, administrators and subscription coordinators, we are phasing out usernames as login credentials. Please switch to using your email address to log into your programs.
  • For Mathetics users, please note: you will not be able to use the same email address for an ADMIN and a teacher accounts. Use the +1 hack to use the same email address. If your email is being used as the administrator/subscription coordinator email, you can add yourself as a teacher using the +1 hack. For example: email+1@nswschool.com.
  • Update your school roster:
    • Remove any students that are no longer at your school. For a step-by-step tutorial visit the Reading Eggs/Mathletics/Mathseeds
      knowledge bases
    • Remove any teachers that are no longer at your school. For instructions visit the Reading Eggs/Mathletics/Mathseeds
      knowledge bases
    • Remove any classes that are no longer being used. Visit the Reading Eggs/Mathletics/Mathseeds
      knowledge bases
