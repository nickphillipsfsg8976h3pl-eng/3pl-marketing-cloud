Speaking of Problem-Solving and Reasoning…


 

Mathletics has over 700 Problem-Solving and Reasoning (PSR) Activities. These are rich learning tasks, designed to help students master soft skills, develop critical thinking abilities and show them how math can be applied to real life problem-solving.

Students can show their work, explain their thinking and ask for help in real time! You can assign PSR Activities for independent work or homework, and students can ask questions or request feedback instantly. Assign PSR Activities from the ‘Assign & Review’ section.
