Welcome to Term 1!

 

With one in three children failing to meet NAPLAN literacy benchmarks, there are calls for a reading revolution. As with every new syllabus, we know there’s pressure on teachers to understand what’s new and what changes are required.

 

A big focus of the NSW Syllabus is best practice, evidence-based science of reading and teaching research. The syllabus sets out The Foundational Big Six of reading: phonemic awareness, phonics, vocabulary, comprehension, fluency, and oral language – each a key focus of our product ABC Reading Eggs.
