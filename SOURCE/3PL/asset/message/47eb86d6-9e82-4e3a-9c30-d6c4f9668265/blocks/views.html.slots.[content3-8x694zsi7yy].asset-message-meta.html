Keeping older students excited about maths doesn’t have to be hard! Here are three easy ways to spark their interest and make maths lessons more engaging for students aged 9–13+:
 

1. Add some friendly competition

Students love a challenge! Whether it’s live maths competitions or weekly tasks, a bit of friendly rivalry can make all the difference. With Mathletics, you can easily set up challenges, track progress and reward success – keeping students motivated every step of the way.
 

2. Keep lessons fresh

Maths is everywhere, from sports stats to budgeting and design. Bring it to life with real-world examples and interactive tools. Mathletics makes it simple to mix things up with gamified activities, creative problem-solving tasks and engaging content your students will love.
 

3. Differentiate without the hassle

Catering to different ability levels doesn’t have to be time-consuming. With Mathletics, grouping students and assigning tailored activities takes just a few clicks. Keep every student engaged with work that’s just right for them.
