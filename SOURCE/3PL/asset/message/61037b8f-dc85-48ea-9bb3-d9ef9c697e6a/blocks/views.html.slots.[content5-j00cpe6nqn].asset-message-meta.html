What do teachers say about Mathletics?

 

“The kids love Mathletics. They love engaging with all the games and the Multiverse, and even enjoy the lessons! They are always eager to play each other and collect points. The site is also great for teachers, the planning and teaching stuff on the site is always really useful and we love the problem-solving resources.”

 

Amy K, Teacher - Leadhills Primary
