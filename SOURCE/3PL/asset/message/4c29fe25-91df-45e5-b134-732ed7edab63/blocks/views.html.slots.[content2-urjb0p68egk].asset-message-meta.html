Starting the Placement Test for the first time
 

The test will launch itself the first time your students enter any of the programs and all they need to do is select 'Do the test'.
 

This will assess their current skills and knowledge and automatically place them at the right starting point in the program. You can see a guide to what is assessed for Fast Phonics here and Reading Eggs and Reading Eggspress here.
