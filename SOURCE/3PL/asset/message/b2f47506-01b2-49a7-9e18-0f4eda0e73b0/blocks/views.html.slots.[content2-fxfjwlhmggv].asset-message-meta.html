What: The Reading Eggspedition is a two-week online literacy event for students aged 5-13. Each year has a special theme, and if you haven't guessed yet - this year we're embarking on an ocean voyage!
 

For these 2 weeks (and beyond) your students will get to class eager to start reading and working on achieving their weekly adventure goals. There will be a prize pack up for grabs each week!
