You’ll soon be able to set up your students in Writing Legends through your Mathseeds account by following these simple steps:
  1. Log in to your Mathseeds Teacher Dashboard
  2. Click on the ‘Writing’ tab located at the top left of the dashboard
  3. In the Navigation panel, click on 'Management' then 'Manage Students'
  4. Select 'Copy Classes' then click 'Copy'
  5. You and your students can enjoy free access
 

Save the date, 9th October and prepare for an epic writing adventure!
